## AI Model Quality Comparison

| Competitor | AI Engine | Quality | Best At | Setup Time |
|-----------|-----------|:---:|---------|:---:|
| **Zipchat** | GPT-4 (OpenAI) | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Natural conversation, sales persuasion, multi-language | ~5 min (auto) |
| **Tidio (Lyro)** | Claude-based proprietary | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Balanced support + sales, knowledge-grounded | 30-60 min (manual) |
| **Gorgias AI** | LLM proprietary | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Structured tasks (returns, order edits) | 1-2 hours |
| **Juphy** | GPT-4 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Product discovery, shopping assistant | ~10 min (auto) |
| **AskShop.ai** | GPT-based | ⭐⭐⭐ | Simple Q&A, visual search | ~5 min (auto) |
| **Ochatbot** | NLP + GPT hybrid | ⭐⭐⭐ | Rule-based reliability with AI fallback | ~20 min |

**Key trend:** Auto-training ("install and it works") is the new standard. Manual FAQ/KB setup is a competitive disadvantage.

## Table-Stakes vs Differentiators for an All-in-One Platform

### 🏁 Table-Stakes (Must-Have on Day 1)

**Livechat:**
1. Real-time chat widget (customizable colors, position)
2. Mobile app (iOS + Android)
3. Offline messages
4. Canned responses / macros
5. Basic customization (brand colors, avatars, welcome message)
6. Notification system (sound, desktop, push)
7. Chat history (searchable archive)

**AI Chatbot:**
8. GPT-level AI quality (natural, human-like responses)
9. Auto-training from store content (products, pages, policies)
10. Product recommendations (contextual)
11. FAQ automation
12. Multi-language (auto-detect + respond)
13. Human handoff (seamless escalation)
14. No-code setup (install → works in minutes)

**Helpdesk:**
15. Shared inbox (unified view)
16. Email integration
17. Basic ticketing (assign, tag, status, priority)
18. Internal notes
19. Basic automation rules (auto-assign, auto-tag)
20. Knowledge base / FAQ
21. Basic reporting (volume, response time)
22. Collision detection

**Shopify Integration:**
23. Customer profile sidebar (order data)
24. Order history in chat
25. Cart contents visible to agents

## Feature Gaps and Opportunities for Chatty

### What's Missing in the Market

| Gap | Opportunity for Chatty |
|-----|----------------------|
| No all-in-one with generous free tier | Offer livechat + AI + helpdesk free (100 AI conversations, 2 agents, 50 tickets) |
| Order actions only in Gorgias ($300+/mo) | Offer order edit/refund/cancel at $79/mo |
| AI always an expensive add-on | Include AI at every tier (not a bolt-on) |
| Multi-channel gated behind premium | Include WhatsApp + IG + email at $29/mo |
| Complex pricing models | One flat price, no surprises |
| Slow setup (30-60 min for Tidio, 1-2 hr for Gorgias) | 5-minute auto-setup (like Zipchat) |
| No strong Shopify-native helpdesk under $30/mo | Fill the gap between Shopify Inbox ($0) and Gorgias ($300+) |

### Competitive Feature Parity Needed

To compete, Chatty must match these specific competitors on specific dimensions:
- **Zipchat's AI quality** — GPT-4 level, auto-training, multi-language
- **Gorgias's Shopify depth** — order actions, revenue attribution (at lower price)
- **Crisp's pricing model** — flat per-workspace, not per-agent
- **Tidio's breadth** — livechat + chatbot + basic helpdesk
- **Shopify Inbox's simplicity** — zero-friction onboarding
