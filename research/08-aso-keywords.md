1. **"AI" is table stakes** in every category — every competitor includes it in title
2. **Feature combos work**: "AI + Helpdesk + Chat" or "Live Chat + AI Chatbot"
3. **Keyword repetition works**: Zipchat's aggressive "AI Chat & AI Chatbot" ranks well
4. **"Free plan available" badge** significantly impacts click-through
5. **Subtitles emphasize outcomes**: "grow your business", "convert more customers", "automate sales"
6. **Action verbs standard**: "Monitor", "Offer", "Boost", "Get", "Convert"
7. **Review count matters**: Tidio (1,158) >> Gorgias (618) >> Re:amaze (177) >> Zipchat (146) >> Zendesk (86)

## 3. Unified ASO Strategy for Chatty as All-in-One App

### Scenario A: Single App (Live Chat + AI + Helpdesk Combined)

**Recommended App Title:**
> **Chatty: Live Chat, AI Chatbot & Helpdesk**

Rationale:
- Hits the #1 keyword from each category in one title
- "Live Chat" first (highest search volume of all keywords)
- "AI Chatbot" second (fast-growing category)
- "Helpdesk" third (captures support/ticketing searchers)
- Brand first, clean and non-spammy

**Recommended Subtitle:**
> "AI-powered customer support — live chat, ticketing & shared inbox for Shopify"

Key elements:
- "AI-powered" — table stakes modifier
- "customer support" — high-volume cross-category keyword
- "live chat" — primary category keyword
- "ticketing" — helpdesk differentiator
- "shared inbox" — growing keyword, low competition
- "for Shopify" — platform-specific intent

## 5. Shopify App Store Ranking Factors

1. **Keyword relevance** in title > subtitle > description (weighted in that order)
2. **Install velocity** — recent installs matter more than total
3. **Review rating + volume** — 4.5+ with 100+ reviews is competitive threshold
4. **Uninstall rate** — low churn signals quality
5. **Merchant engagement** — active usage post-install
6. **Built for Shopify badge** — significant ranking boost if eligible
7. **Recency of updates** — recently updated apps rank higher
8. **"Free plan available" badge** — impacts click-through rate significantly

## 6. Review Strategy

- Encourage reviews mentioning keywords across all 3 categories (live chat, AI chatbot, helpdesk)
- In-app prompt after: successful live chat conversations, AI resolving a query, ticket closure
- Target 4.8+ rating — match Zipchat, beat Tidio
- Respond to every negative review publicly (builds trust)
- Aim for 200+ reviews within first 6 months
- Review responses should naturally mention "live chat", "AI chatbot", "helpdesk", "Shopify"
