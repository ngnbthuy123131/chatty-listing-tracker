# Consolidated Competitor Pricing Analysis — All Categories

*Last updated: 2026-02-23 | Covers: Livechat, AI Chatbot, Helpdesk*

---

## Pricing Model Comparison

| Model | Description | Who Uses It | Pros | Cons |
|-------|-------------|-------------|------|------|
| **Per-conversation** | Charge per AI/chat conversation | Tidio (Lyro) | Scales with value | Unpredictable costs |
| **Per-resolution** | Charge only when AI fully resolves | Gorgias AI, Intercom (Fin), Help Scout | Pay for outcomes | Costs still unpredictable, additive |
| **Per-ticket** | Charge per support ticket | Gorgias (base) | Scales with volume | Penalizes growth, surprise bills |
| **Per-agent/seat** | Charge per support agent | LiveChat, Re:amaze, Zendesk, Freshdesk, Help Scout, Chatra, Front, Gladly, Kustomer | Predictable per agent | Expensive when scaling team |
| **Per-order** | Charge per Shopify order | Richpanel | Ties to business metric | Penalizes high-volume stores |
| **Flat monthly** | Fixed price per tier | Zipchat, Juphy, Crisp, AskShop.ai | Simple, predictable | May not scale efficiently |
| **Free forever** | Fully free product | Shopify Inbox, Tawk.to | Zero barrier | Monetization via other means |
| **Hybrid** | Mix of models | Tidio (base + add-ons), Richpanel (orders + seats) | Flexible | Confusing |

## Pricing Model Verdict

### What's Working
- **Flat monthly with clear limits** (Zipchat, Juphy, Crisp) — merchants prefer predictable costs
- **Free tier** (Tidio, Juphy, AskShop, Help Scout) — critical for adoption
- **Self-serve pricing** — no "contact sales" barrier for SMBs
- **Per-workspace flat rate** (Crisp) — disruptive vs per-agent

### What's NOT Working
- **Per-ticket** (Gorgias) — unpredictable, creates billing anxiety, biggest merchant complaint
- **Per-resolution add-ons** (Gorgias AI, Intercom) — costs additive and unpredictable
- **Complex add-on stacking** (Tidio) — base + Lyro + Flows = confusing total
- **Per-agent at high prices** (Zendesk $55+, LiveChat $41+) — penalizes team growth
- **No free plan** (Zipchat, Gorgias, LiveChat) — higher barrier to adoption

## Chatty Recommended Pricing Strategy

### Positioning: "Generous Free + Flat Predictable Paid"

| Tier | Suggested Price | What's Included | Rationale |
|------|----------------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Livechat (2 agents) + 100 AI conversations/mo + basic helpdesk (50 tickets/mo) | More generous than Tidio (50 lifetime AI), Juphy (~50/mo), Crisp (100 profiles). Competitive with Shopify Inbox + meaningful AI. |
| **Starter** | $29/mo | Livechat (5 agents) + 500 AI conversations/mo + helpdesk (unlimited tickets) + email channel | Undercut Zipchat ($49, no free), match AskShop.ai value, beat Tidio on simplicity |
| **Growth** | $79/mo | Unlimited agents + 2,000 AI conversations/mo + all channels (WhatsApp, IG, Messenger, email) + advanced analytics + order actions | Match Juphy Scale ($199) at lower price, compete with Re:amaze Basic ($29×3 agents = $87) |
| **Pro** | $199/mo | Unlimited everything + 10,000 AI conversations/mo + AI auto-resolution + revenue attribution + multi-brand + SLA management | Undercut Gorgias Pro ($300+AI) massively. Clear enterprise path. |

### Key Pricing Principles

1. **Generous free tier** — 100 free AI conversations/mo across livechat + chatbot + helpdesk. Strongest free offer in market.
2. **Flat monthly pricing** — no per-conversation, per-ticket, or per-agent surprises. One price, all features.
3. **Include all channels at every paid tier** — like Zipchat, unlike Tidio/Gorgias who gate channels behind higher tiers.
4. **No add-on complexity** — one price includes everything (AI, livechat, helpdesk). Anti-Tidio positioning.
5. **Target the $29–$79 sweet spot** — where Tidio is overpriced/confusing and Zipchat has no free option.
6. **Annual discount: 20-25%** — standard across market.
7. **AI as included, not add-on** — key differentiator vs Tidio (Lyro add-on), Gorgias ($0.90/resolution), Help Scout ($0.75/resolution).
