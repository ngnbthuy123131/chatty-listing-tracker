# Step 3 — Livechat Competitor Deep Dive

*Last updated: 2026-02-23*

## Market Overview

The Shopify livechat space is crowded and increasingly commoditized. The biggest competitive threat is **Shopify Inbox** — free, native, and pre-installed. Most third-party apps now bundle AI chatbots alongside livechat, blurring the pure livechat category.

---

## Top Competitors on Shopify

### 1. Shopify Inbox (Native) ⚠️ PRIMARY THREAT
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Rating | 4.7 ⭐ |
| Reviews | ~5,244 |
| Price | **FREE** (built into Shopify) |
| Installs | Massive — pre-installed on all Shopify stores |

**Strengths:**
- Zero cost, zero friction — available to every Shopify merchant by default
- Deep native Shopify integration (order data, cart contents, customer info)
- Mobile app for on-the-go responses
- Suggested replies and quick answers
- Product sharing directly in chat
- Automatic greeting messages

**Weaknesses:**
- Basic feature set — no agent routing, no departments
- Limited customization of chat widget
- No multi-channel (website only)
- No advanced analytics or reporting
- No canned responses library management
- No visitor tracking/real-time visitor list
- No typing indicators for agents
- No file sharing from visitors
- No chat ratings

**Strategic Impact:** Shopify Inbox sets the floor. Any paid livechat app must clearly justify its cost above what merchants get for free. This is the #1 reason many small merchants never install a third-party chat app.

### 2. Tidio
| Metric | Value |

| Reviews | 1,158 |
| Shopify App | [tidio-chat](https://apps.shopify.com/tidio-chat) |

**Strengths:**

**Weaknesses:**

- Some merchants report slow support

| Formilla | 4.9 | 19 | ✅ Limited | Freemium |

### Key Competitive Dynamics

1. **Free vs Paid**: Shopify Inbox + Tawk.to create massive downward price pressure. Any paid app must demonstrably exceed free alternatives.

2. **AI Convergence**: Nearly every competitor is pivoting to AI chatbots. Pure livechat is becoming a commodity feature within larger platforms.

3. **Consolidation**: JivoChat, Olark, Willdesk have exited/been removed from Shopify. Chatra acquired by Brevo. Market is consolidating.

4. **Per-Agent vs Per-Workspace**: Crisp's flat pricing model is disruptive vs. per-agent pricing (LiveChat, Chatra). Tidio moved to per-conversation billing.

5. **SMB Sweet Spot**: The $0-30/mo range is where most Shopify merchants shop. Apps above $50/mo serve enterprise only.
