## 3. Recommended ASO Strategy for Chatty

### A. Title Optimization

**Current:** `Chatty AI Chatbot & Live Chat`

**Recommended options (prioritizing live chat):**

1. `Chatty: Live Chat & AI Chatbot` — Swap order, lead with live chat
2. `Chatty ‑ Live Chat, WhatsApp & AI` — Add channel keyword
3. `Chatty: Live Chat Widget & AI Bot` — Add "widget" keyword

**Rationale:** "Live chat" has higher search volume than "AI chatbot" for the target niche. Leading with it captures more live-chat-specific searches while keeping AI as secondary differentiator.

### B. Subtitle Optimization

**Current:** "Boost customer support with livechat by AI chat bot and..."

**Recommended:**
> "Live chat widget for real-time customer support, WhatsApp & FAQ"

**Why:** Packs primary keywords (live chat, widget, real-time, customer support, WhatsApp, FAQ) into one line. Clear value prop.

### C. Description Strategy

Structure the long description in this keyword-optimized order:

1. **Opening paragraph** — Hit primary keywords: "live chat", "chat widget", "Shopify store", "real-time", "customer support"
2. **Feature bullets** — Each bullet = one keyword cluster:
   - Live chat with visitors in real-time
   - Customizable chat widget matching your brand
   - WhatsApp & social media chat integration
   - AI chatbot for automated responses
   - FAQ & help center
   - Mobile app for chat on the go
   - Cart & order data in chat sidebar
3. **Benefits section** — "Increase sales", "reduce response time", "boost customer satisfaction"
4. **Integration mentions** — Shopify, WhatsApp, Messenger, email
5. **Closing CTA** — "Free plan available" / "Install in seconds"

### D. Category & Tag Strategy

**Primary category:** Store Management > Customer Support
**Tags to claim:** live chat, chat widget, customer chat, live support, WhatsApp chat

### E. Visual Assets (Screenshots)

Optimize screenshot order for live chat positioning:
1. **Hero:** Chat widget on a beautiful Shopify store (mobile + desktop)
2. **Chat inbox:** Clean, modern conversation view
3. **Mobile app:** Merchant replying from phone
4. **Customization:** Widget color/position/style options
5. **Shopify integration:** Order data visible in chat sidebar
6. **WhatsApp/social:** Multi-channel inbox

### F. Review Strategy

- Encourage live chat-specific reviews (not just AI chatbot)
- In-app prompt after successful chat conversations: "How was your chat experience?"
- Target keywords in review responses: naturally mention "live chat", "widget", "Shopify"
