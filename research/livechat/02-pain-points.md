# Step 2 — Pain Points & Opportunities: Live Chat Apps on Shopify

*Research date: 2026-02-23 | Sources: Trustpilot 1-star reviews, Shopify App Store reviews, Reddit r/shopify*

---

## 1. Top Pain Points by Category

### 🔴 Billing, Pricing & Cancellation (CRITICAL — #1 complaint across all apps)

| App | Issue |
|-----|-------|
| **LiveChat** | Charged $805 without consent; charged after cancellation two years in a row; can't cancel subscription without contacting support; per-operator pricing model called "greedy" |
| **Tidio** | Doubled rates without notification, charged for full year; won't cancel account even after explicit request; free trial → paid confusion |
| **Crisp** | Extremely difficult to cancel (may violate EU consumer law); lack of transparency in pricing |
| **tawk.to** | Auto-renewed yearly subscription with no use, refused refund; kept deducting after confirmed cancellation |

**Pattern:** Merchants universally hate surprise charges, auto-renewals after cancellation, and opaque cancellation flows. Per-seat/per-operator pricing is a sore point for growing teams.

### 🟠 Customer Support Quality (Ironic — chat companies with bad support)

| App | Issue |
|-----|-------|
| **Tidio** | "Appalling customer service — no response for 2 weeks"; scheduled meeting rep didn't attend; AI support can't help with real issues |
| **Crisp** | "Never ever use this — customer support is so poor, no one responds on time"; multi-day waits for screenshots; support lacks basic technical understanding |
| **LiveChat** | Slow, frustrating process requiring screenshots/card details for their own billing errors |
| **tawk.to** | "13 stages to set up. No telephone help. Each message takes ~7 minutes to reply" |

**Pattern:** Chat support companies that can't provide decent chat support themselves. Merchants are furious about the hypocrisy.

### 🟡 Account Suspensions & Trust Issues

| App | Issue |
|-----|-------|
| **Tidio** | Multiple reports of accounts suspended without notice after months/years of use; "no explanation"; automated flagging with no human review; suspended twice in one month after 3-year relationship |
| **Crisp** | Accounts suspended within hours/days of setup during testing; suspended for "unverifiable" websites; inconsistent enforcement (bigger companies fly under radar) |
| **tawk.to** | Phishing scam alert on knowledge base pages; platform used by scammers with no due diligence |

**Pattern:** Automated moderation suspending legitimate merchants without warning or explanation. Devastating for businesses relying on live chat for customer support.

### 🟢 Mobile UX & Widget Problems

| App | Issue |
|-----|-------|
| **tawk.to** | "Do Not Disturb" mode re-enables itself on mobile, causing missed chats; widget expands and covers ENTIRE website on mobile with no way to change it |
| **Crisp** | Constant reloads, freezes, broken features "like something still in early testing" |
| **LiveChat** | AI feature makes UX worse — asks same question repeatedly before connecting to human |

**Pattern:** Mobile experience is afterthought. Widgets are intrusive on small screens. Mobile notification reliability is critical and often broken.

### 🔵 Performance & Reliability

| App | Issue |
|-----|-------|
| **tawk.to** | "Real time not working"; platform agent performance is low |
| **Crisp** | Platform freezes, constant reloads, broken features became the norm |
| **Tidio** | Chatbot doesn't automatically populate answers — requires clicking each time |

**Pattern:** Core real-time functionality fails. If the chat widget is slow or unreliable, the entire value proposition collapses.

### 🟣 AI Feature Frustration (Adjacent but relevant)

| App | Issue |
|-----|-------|
| **Crisp** | AI answers off-topic/inaccurate despite weeks of training; PDF data source silently cropped; AI workflows deactivated without notice when new AI agent launched; escalation messages not customizable |
| **LiveChat** | AI chatbot asks same question in loops; "useless AI feature that makes UX worse" |
| **Tidio** | Chatbot requires manual click for each answer; Lyro AI can barely answer help questions |

**Pattern:** Merchants forced into AI features they don't want. AI gets between visitor and human agent. This is an opportunity for Chatty: **position live chat as the core, AI as optional enhancement**.

## 2. Pain Points from Reddit & Shopify Community

### From Reddit r/shopify (Gorgias Alternative thread + others):
- **Pricing scales painfully**: Gorgias at ~$500/mo for AI + $200/mo for voice = $700+/mo for mid-size store
- Merchants want **simple, affordable live chat** without being forced into full helpdesk suites
- Integration with Shopify order data is valued but shouldn't require expensive plans
- Small merchants (1-5 person teams) feel priced out by per-seat models

### Common Community Complaints:
- Widget slows down storefront (PageSpeed impact)
- Limited customization of chat widget appearance to match brand
- Notification reliability — missed chats = lost sales
- No native WhatsApp/social integration on affordable plans
- Chat history lost between sessions for returning visitors

## 3. Unmet Needs & Whitespace Opportunities

### 🎯 Clear Whitespace for Chatty

1. **Transparent, simple pricing** — No per-seat traps, no hidden fees, no auto-renewal gotchas. Flat pricing or generous free tier. This alone differentiates.

2. **Easy cancellation** — One-click uninstall cancels everything. Promote this as a feature. Trust = retention.

3. **Lightweight widget** — Competitors add bloat (AI, helpdesk, ticketing). A fast, minimal chat widget that doesn't tank PageSpeed scores.

4. **Mobile-first design** — Widget that's responsive, non-intrusive on mobile, reliable notifications on iOS/Android.

5. **Human-first, AI-optional** — Don't force AI between visitor and merchant. Let merchants chat directly with customers. AI should be an opt-in enhancement, not a gate.

6. **Responsive support for a support app** — Ironic differentiator: actually answer your own support tickets quickly. Competitors are terrible at this.

7. **No surprise suspensions** — Clear terms, human review before account actions. Small merchants are terrified of losing their support channel overnight.

8. **Shopify-native integration** — Cart contents, order history, customer data visible in chat without complex setup. Competitors charge premium for this.

9. **Offline message handling** — When merchant is away, graceful fallback (contact form, WhatsApp redirect) instead of dead chat widget.

10. **Multi-language widget** — Many Shopify stores serve international customers. Auto-detect language for chat widget.

### Key Takeaway
The live chat market on Shopify is ripe for disruption. Incumbents are moving upmarket (AI, helpdesk suites) and neglecting core live chat quality. Merchants want **simple, reliable, affordable real-time chat** — and current options are failing them on billing trust, support quality, and mobile UX. Chatty's opportunity is to be the "anti-enterprise" live chat: lightweight, honest, Shopify-native.
