## Price Sensitivity Analysis

### The Free Tier Arms Race
| App | Free Plan Quality | Risk to Chatty |
|-----|-------------------|----------------|
| Shopify Inbox | ★★★★★ (full-featured, native) | 🔴 Critical |
| Tawk.to | ★★★★☆ (unlimited but buggy) | 🟡 Medium |
| Crisp | ★★★☆☆ (2 agents, 100 profiles) | 🟢 Low |
| Tidio | ★★☆☆☆ (50 conversations only) | 🟢 Low |
| Chatra | ★☆☆☆☆ (1 agent, 5 chats) | 🟢 Low |

### Key Pricing Insights

1. **$0 is the real competitor.** Shopify Inbox is free and "good enough" for 60-70% of merchants. Any paid livechat must offer 3-5x more value.

2. **Sweet spot: $15-30/mo.** Shopify merchants are accustomed to paying $10-30/mo for apps. Above $50/mo, the market shrinks dramatically.

3. **Per-agent pricing penalizes growth.** At $25-41/agent, a 5-person team pays $125-205/mo — painful for small stores. Crisp's flat model is disruptive.

4. **Conversation-based billing (Tidio) is unpredictable.** Merchants dislike variable costs. Flat pricing wins trust.

5. **Tawk.to's model validates:** Pure livechat software is hard to monetize. They give it away and sell human agent services instead.

6. **AI is the new upsell lever.** Tidio, LiveChat, Crisp all use AI features to justify higher tiers. This is where margin lives.

### Pricing Strategy Implications for Chatty

- **Free tier must be competitive** with Shopify Inbox (not just a crippled trial)
- **Paid tier should target $15-25/mo** to sit below LiveChat/Tidio but above free
- **Avoid per-agent pricing** — follow Crisp's flat model or include 3-5 agents
- **AI/automation as premium upsell** is the proven path to higher ARPU
- **Annual discount** of 20-30% is standard across all competitors
