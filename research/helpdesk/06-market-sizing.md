- Many small stores use email + Shopify Inbox (free)

- Awareness gap: many merchants don't know helpdesk tools exist until they hit pain points

### Market Growth Drivers
1. **Shopify merchant base growing** at ~15–20% annually
2. **Helpdesk adoption increasing** as stores mature and support volume grows
3. **AI add-ons expanding ARPU** (new revenue stream on top of base subscription)
4. **Omnichannel expansion** (WhatsApp, TikTok Shop) creating new ticket sources

### Market Growth Headwinds
1. **AI deflection reducing ticket volumes** (fewer tickets = less revenue on usage-based models)
2. **Consolidation** — fewer but larger players
3. **Platform risk** — Shopify could build native support features
4. **Klaviyo entry** may commoditize basic helpdesk

## 5. ARPU Analysis: Helpdesk vs Livechat

| Metric | Helpdesk Apps | Livechat Apps | Multiple |
|--------|--------------|---------------|----------|
| **Average ARPU/mo** | $150–400 | $10–30 | **10–15x** |
| **Median ARPU/mo** | ~$200 | ~$15 | **~13x** |
| **Top-tier ARPU** | $500–2,000+ | $50–100 | **10–20x** |
| **Free tier usage** | ~20–30% | ~60–80% | — |

### Why Helpdesk ARPU Is Much Higher

1. **Mission-critical tool** — helpdesk is the operational backbone of support teams; livechat is optional
2. **Multi-agent usage** — helpdesk serves entire teams (3–20+ agents); livechat often 1–2 people
3. **Ticket volume pricing** — usage-based pricing scales with business growth
4. **Multiple channels** — email + social + SMS + voice = higher value than a single chat widget
5. **Higher switching costs** — migrating ticketing history, workflows, macros is painful
6. **Enterprise demand** — larger stores need SLA, reporting, CSAT → willing to pay premium

### ARPU by Segment
| Segment | Helpdesk ARPU/mo | % of Market |
|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| Starter (1–100 orders/mo) | $10–50 | ~40% of installs, ~5% of revenue |
| Growth (100–1,000 orders/mo) | $100–300 | ~35% of installs, ~30% of revenue |
| Scale (1,000–10,000 orders/mo) | $300–900 | ~20% of installs, ~40% of revenue |
| Enterprise (10,000+ orders/mo) | $1,000–5,000+ | ~5% of installs, ~25% of revenue |

### Addressable Gap for Chatty
| Segment | Opportunity | Why |
|---------|------------|-----|
| **Underserved SMBs** (50–500 orders/mo) | ~$50–100M unrealized | Too small for Gorgias Pro, too big for email only |
| **Price-sensitive merchants** | ~$30–50M | Gorgias expensive at scale; want $20–50/mo option |
| **Existing Chatty livechat users** | Upsell path | Natural progression: livechat → helpdesk as store grows |
| **Emerging markets** | ~$20–40M | India, SEA, LATAM underserved by Gorgias |

| Scenario | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|----------|--------|--------|--------|
| Conservative | $0.5M | $2M | $5M |

**Key assumption:** Chatty can leverage existing livechat install base to cross-sell helpdesk at lower price points than Gorgias, targeting the 50–500 orders/mo segment.
