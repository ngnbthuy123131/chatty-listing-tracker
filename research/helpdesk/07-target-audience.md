# Step 7 — Target Audience: Helpdesk Niche

## Who Needs a Shopify Helpdesk?

Merchants who have **outgrown email/livechat** and need:
- **Ticket management** — assign, prioritize, track, resolve across a team
- **Multi-channel inbox** — email + social (FB, IG, TikTok) + chat + SMS/voice unified
- **Automation** — macros, rules, auto-routing, canned responses
- **Team collaboration** — multiple agents, roles, departments, shifts
- **Reporting** — SLA tracking, agent performance, CSAT, resolution times
- **Deep Shopify integration** — view/edit orders, refunds, customer history in-ticket

### Trigger Points (When Merchants Need Helpdesk)
- **>50 tickets/week** — solo founder can't keep up with email alone
- **>2 support agents** — need assignment, collision detection, shared inbox
- **Multi-channel support** — customers contacting via email AND social AND chat
- **Return/refund volume** — need workflows for order actions
- **SLA requirements** — B2B or premium brands promising response times

## Segments

### 1. Solo Merchants (1 person, <$50K/mo revenue)
- **Need:** Minimal. Email + basic livechat suffices
- **Helpdesk adoption:** Very low. Overkill for their volume
- **WTP:** $0–15/mo max for any support tool
- **Verdict:** NOT a helpdesk target. These merchants need livechat/FAQ only

### 2. SMB (2–10 people, $50K–$500K/mo revenue)
- **Need:** Growing. 100–500 tickets/month, 2–5 support agents
- **Helpdesk adoption:** Moderate. Many use shared Gmail or basic tools
- **Pain points:** Losing track of tickets, slow response times, no reporting
- **WTP:** $50–200/mo — price-sensitive but willing to pay for efficiency
- **Key insight:** This is the **underserved gap**. Gorgias starts at ticket-based pricing that gets expensive. Re:amaze at $29/agent/mo is more accessible but less Shopify-native

### 3. Mid-Market (10–50 people, $500K–$5M/mo revenue)
- **Need:** High. 500–5,000+ tickets/month, dedicated support team
- **Helpdesk adoption:** Near universal. Running Gorgias, Re:amaze, or Zendesk
- **WTP:** $200–1,000/mo — budget allocated for CX tooling
- **Verdict:** Gorgias's sweet spot. Hard to displace

### 4. Enterprise / Shopify Plus ($5M+/mo revenue)
- **Need:** Critical. Thousands of tickets, complex workflows, SLA enforcement
- **Helpdesk adoption:** 100%. Running Gorgias Enterprise, Zendesk, or Salesforce
- **WTP:** $1,000–10,000+/mo
- **Verdict:** NOT a realistic target for Chatty. Requires years of feature parity

## Willingness to Pay

Helpdesk has the **highest ARPU** in the Shopify support app category:

| App | Pricing Model | Effective Monthly Cost |
|-----|--------------|----------------------|
| **Gorgias** | Ticket-based: 50 tickets free → $40/100 overage → tiered plans for 300/2K/5K+ tickets | $0 (tiny) to $750+ (mid-market) |
| **Re:amaze** | Per-agent: $29–69/agent/mo OR $59 flat (starter, 500 convos) | $59–$500+ |
| **Zendesk** | Per-agent: $19–115/agent/mo | $100–$1,000+ |
| **Freshdesk** | Per-agent: Free (10 agents) → $15–79/agent/mo | $0–$500+ |
| **Tidio** | Hybrid: Free → $29–39/mo + per-seat | $0–200 |

**Key observation:** Gorgias's ticket-based pricing scales aggressively. A store with 1,000 tickets/month is paying $300+/mo. This creates **price pain** for growing SMBs.

## The SMB Gap Opportunity

### Gorgias Targets Mid-Market+
- Gorgias is a Shopify Plus premier partner (exclusive relationship)
- Their ideal customer: 500+ tickets/mo, $1M+ revenue, dedicated CX team
- Their ticket-based pricing **punishes growth** — costs scale linearly with volume
- 9% 1-star reviews on Shopify App Store (618 total reviews, 4.3 stars) — pricing complaints common

### The Gap
- **Below Gorgias:** Merchants with 100–500 tickets/month, 2–5 agents
- **Above basic livechat:** Merchants who need ticket management but not a full CX platform
- **Price-sensitive SMBs:** Want helpdesk features at $20–50/mo, not $200+/mo
- **Shopify-native simplicity:** Don't want to configure Zendesk for their 3-person team

### What This Segment Wants
1. **Shared inbox** with ticket assignment (not just livechat)
2. **Order sidebar** — see customer orders, issue refunds without leaving the ticket
3. **Basic automation** — auto-tag, auto-assign, canned responses
4. **Multi-channel** — at minimum email + chat + social DMs
5. **Simple pricing** — flat rate or per-agent, NOT per-ticket
6. **Quick setup** — working in 15 minutes, not 2 days

### Market Size Estimate
- ~2M+ active Shopify stores globally
- ~5–10% have support needs beyond basic chat (~100K–200K stores)
- ~50% of those are SMBs underserved by current helpdesk pricing (~50K–100K stores)
- At $30/mo average → **$18M–36M/year TAM** for SMB helpdesk on Shopify
- Realistic capture (5–10% over 2–3 years) → **$1M–3.6M/year revenue potential**
