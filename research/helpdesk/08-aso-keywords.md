## Competitor Listing Analysis

### Gorgias (slug: `/helpdesk`)
- **Title**: "Gorgias: AI, Helpdesk & Chat"
- **Subtitle**: "Instantly resolve support inquiries and grow your business."
- **Keywords in title**: AI, Helpdesk, Chat
- **Strategy**: Brand-first, category second; owns the `/helpdesk` slug (massive SEO advantage)
- **Rating**: 4.3★ (618 reviews)
- **Pricing display**: "Free trial available" — no free plan

### Zendesk (slug: `/zendesk`)
- **Title**: "Zendesk"
- **Subtitle**: "Omnichannel customer service and engagement platform"
- **Keywords in title**: Just brand name
- **Keywords in subtitle**: Omnichannel, customer service, engagement platform
- **Strategy**: Pure brand recognition play; relies on name alone
- **Rating**: 3.3★ (86 reviews)
- **Pricing**: External billing — red flag for merchants

### Re:amaze (slug: `/reamaze`)
- **Title**: "Re:amaze AI Helpdesk & Chat"
- **Subtitle**: "Convert more customers w/ AI powered helpdesk and live chat"
- **Keywords in title**: AI, Helpdesk, Chat
- **Keywords in subtitle**: AI powered, helpdesk, live chat, convert customers
- **Strategy**: Keyword-rich; mirrors Gorgias pattern; adds conversion angle
- **Rating**: 4.4★ (177 reviews)
- **Pricing**: "Free plan available" ← key differentiator in listings

### Patterns Observed
1. **"AI" is now table stakes** in listing titles — every competitor leads with it
2. **"Helpdesk" + "Chat"** is the dominant title combo
3. **Free plan available** badge significantly impacts click-through (Re:amaze has it, Gorgias doesn't)
4. **Subtitle = value prop**, not feature list — "grow your business", "convert more customers"
5. **Review count matters**: Gorgias (618) >> Re:amaze (177) >> Zendesk (86)
6. Related apps shown have 249-1158 reviews — social proof is critical

## ASO Strategy for Chatty Helpdesk

### Recommended App Title
**"Chatty: AI Helpdesk & Ticketing"**

Rationale:
- Brand first (Chatty already has recognition from livechat app)
- "AI" matches competitor pattern, signals modernity
- "Helpdesk" is the #1 category keyword
- "Ticketing" differentiates from livechat-focused competitors — signals shared inbox/ticket management
- Avoids "Chat" to prevent confusion with Chatty's existing livechat app

### Alternative Titles (test options)
- "Chatty: Helpdesk & Shared Inbox"
- "Chatty: AI Support Desk & Ticketing"
- "Chatty: Customer Support Helpdesk"

### Recommended Subtitle
**"Simple helpdesk with ticketing, shared inbox & Shopify order management"**

Rationale:
- Hits 3 primary keywords: helpdesk, ticketing, shared inbox
- "Simple" positions against bloated competitors (key pain point)
- "Shopify order management" signals deep native integration
- Speaks to SMB merchant who wants simplicity

### Alternative Subtitles
- "Affordable helpdesk for Shopify — ticketing, shared inbox, customer support"
- "Manage customer support tickets & orders in one simple inbox"

### Key Listing Optimization

1. **Pricing badge**: Must have "Free plan available" — this is the #1 competitive advantage over Gorgias
2. **Screenshots priority**:
   - Shared inbox / ticket view with Shopify order sidebar
   - Simple setup flow (counter "complex setup" pain point)
   - Pricing comparison showing value vs competitors
3. **Description keywords to include** (naturally):
   - helpdesk, help desk, customer support, ticketing, ticket management
   - shared inbox, team inbox, support inbox
   - customer service, support desk
   - Shopify orders, order management, refunds
   - multichannel, email support
   - affordable, simple, easy setup
   - small business, SMB
4. **"Featured in" categories** to target:
   - Store management → Customer support
5. **Review velocity**: Prioritize getting to 50+ reviews quickly; competitors with <100 reviews (Zendesk: 86) are vulnerable

### Search Ranking Factors (Shopify App Store)

1. **Keyword match** in title > subtitle > description
2. **Review rating** (aim for 4.8+)
3. **Review count** (velocity matters more than total)
4. **Install count** (free plan drives this)
5. **Engagement signals** (low uninstall rate)
6. **Recency** of reviews
7. **Built for Shopify** badge (if eligible)
