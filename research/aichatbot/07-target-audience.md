# Step 7 — Target Audience: AI Chatbot for Shopify

## Who Needs AI Chatbot Most?

### 1. High-Volume Stores (100+ tickets/day)
- Repetitive queries (order status, shipping, returns) consume support bandwidth
- AI can deflect 40-70% of tickets instantly
- Every $1 spent on AI saves $3-8 in human agent costs at scale

### 2. Stores Without Dedicated Support Teams
- Solo operators and small teams who can't staff 24/7
- AI chatbot = always-on support without hiring
- Critical for stores doing $50K-$500K/yr that can't justify full-time agents

### 3. International / Multilingual Stores
- Selling cross-border but can't hire agents in every language
- AI chatbots handle 50+ languages natively via LLMs
- Especially valuable for stores targeting EU, LATAM, SEA markets

### 4. Stores with High Pre-Sale Question Volume
- Fashion, electronics, supplements — products that generate "will this work for me?" questions
- AI trained on product catalog can convert browsers to buyers
- Direct revenue impact (not just cost savings)

### 5. Stores with Complex Product Catalogs
- Many SKUs, variants, or customization options
- Customers need guided selling / product recommendation
- AI can act as virtual shopping assistant

## Segment Analysis

### Solo Operators ($0-$50K/yr revenue)
- **Profile:** 1 person running everything, Shopify Basic plan
- **Need:** "Set and forget" AI that handles FAQs while they sleep
- **Price sensitivity:** Very high — $0-$19/mo ceiling
- **Willingness to pay for AI:** Low absolute $ but high perceived value (time savings)
- **Key trigger:** Missing sales because they can't respond fast enough

### SMB ($50K-$1M/yr revenue)
- **Profile:** 1-5 person team, possibly 1 part-time support person
- **Need:** Deflect repetitive tickets, free up human for complex issues
- **Price sensitivity:** Moderate — $19-$79/mo acceptable if ROI is clear
- **Willingness to pay:** Medium-high — they feel the pain of support costs
- **Key trigger:** Support costs growing faster than revenue
- **🎯 SWEET SPOT for Chatty** — largest addressable segment on Shopify

### Mid-Market ($1M-$10M/yr revenue)
- **Profile:** 5-30 person team, dedicated support staff (2-5 agents)
- **Need:** Scale support without proportionally scaling headcount
- **Price sensitivity:** Lower — $79-$299/mo is fine if it saves 1 headcount
- **Willingness to pay:** High — clear ROI math against agent salaries
- **Key trigger:** Hiring is slow/expensive, AI can bridge the gap

### Enterprise ($10M+/yr revenue)
- **Profile:** Large team, existing helpdesk (Zendesk/Gorgias), complex workflows
- **Need:** AI layer on top of existing stack, custom training, API access
- **Price sensitivity:** Low — $299-$999+/mo if it integrates well
- **Willingness to pay:** Very high but demands enterprise features (SSO, SLA, compliance)
- **Key trigger:** Board/leadership pushing AI adoption for efficiency
- **Note:** Often already using Gorgias AI, Ada, or Intercom Fin — harder to win

## ROI Calculation: Human Agent vs AI Chatbot

### Cost of Human Support Agent
| Item | Monthly Cost |
|------|-------------|
| Full-time agent (US) | $3,000-$4,500 |
| Full-time agent (Philippines/offshore) | $800-$1,500 |
| Part-time / freelance agent | $500-$2,000 |
| Helpdesk software seat | $50-$100 |
| Training & onboarding (amortized) | $100-$300 |
| **Total per agent** | **$950-$5,400/mo** |

### Typical Agent Productivity
- ~50-80 tickets/day for a trained agent
- ~1,500-2,000 tickets/month per agent
- Average cost per ticket: **$0.50-$3.50**

### AI Chatbot Cost
| Item | Monthly Cost |
|------|-------------|
| AI chatbot subscription (mid-tier) | $49-$149/mo |
| Handles 500-5,000 conversations/mo | Included or usage-based |
| Average cost per AI resolution | **$0.02-$0.15** |

### ROI Math for a Typical SMB Store
- **Scenario:** 1,000 support tickets/month
- **Without AI:** 1 agent at $1,500/mo (offshore) = $1.50/ticket
- **With AI (60% deflection):** 600 tickets handled by AI ($79/mo plan) + 400 by human
  - AI cost: $79/mo
  - Human handles 400 tickets (part-time): ~$600/mo
  - **Total: $679/mo vs $1,500/mo = 55% savings**
  - **Annual savings: ~$9,850**

### ROI Math for Mid-Market
- **Scenario:** 5,000 support tickets/month, 3 agents
- **Without AI:** 3 agents at $4,500/mo total
- **With AI (50% deflection):** 2,500 by AI ($199/mo) + 2,500 by 1.5 agents ($2,250/mo)
  - **Total: $2,449/mo vs $4,500/mo = 46% savings**
  - **Annual savings: ~$24,600**
  - Pays for itself in first week

## Willingness to Pay Summary

| Segment | Monthly Budget | Key Value Prop | Conversion Difficulty |
|---------|---------------|----------------|----------------------|
| Solo | $0-$19 | Time freedom | Easy to convert, low LTV |
| SMB | $19-$79 | Cost savings + speed | Best conversion + LTV balance |
| Mid-Market | $79-$299 | Headcount reduction | Moderate, needs proof |
| Enterprise | $299-$999+ | Scale + compliance | Hard, long sales cycle |

**Primary target for Chatty: SMB segment ($50K-$1M stores)**
- Largest volume on Shopify
- Pain is acute but solutions are affordable
- Self-serve onboarding (no sales team needed)
- $29-$79/mo price point maximizes volume × LTV
