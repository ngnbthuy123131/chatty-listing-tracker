# Step 3 — AI Chatbot Competitor Deep Dive

> Last updated: 2026-02-23 | Focus: AI chatbot for Shopify e-commerce (not general livechat/helpdesk)

---

## Competitive Landscape Overview

The Shopify AI chatbot market has consolidated around a few major players while seeing a wave of GPT-powered newcomers since 2023. Key dynamics:

- **Incumbents** (Tidio, Gorgias) added AI to existing helpdesk/livechat platforms
- **AI-native entrants** (Zipchat, Juphy, AskShop.ai) built AI-first for Shopify
- **Some players have exited**: Gobot (acquired/dormant), Relish AI (delisted), Certainly (pivoted to enterprise), BotStar (shutdown), Heyday (delisted from Shopify App Store after Hootsuite acquisition)
- **Shopify native AI** (Sidekick/Magic) is merchant-facing, not customer-facing — not a direct competitor

## Top Competitors (Active on Shopify as of 2025–2026)

### 1. Tidio (Lyro AI Agent)
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Rating** | 4.7 ⭐ |
| **Reviews** | ~1,158 |
| **Installs (est.)** | 300,000+ |
| **AI Model** | Proprietary (Claude-based Lyro engine) |
| **Shopify URL** | apps.shopify.com/tidio-chat |

**AI Capabilities:**
- Lyro AI Agent: answers from knowledge base, learns from FAQ/help center content
- Product recommendations via conversational AI
- Order status lookups (Shopify integration)
- Multi-language support (6+ languages)
- Human handoff with conversation context
- Actions: can perform tasks (cancel orders, update info) on Premium tier

**Strengths:**
- Massive install base and brand recognition
- Full platform: livechat + chatbot flows + AI agent + helpdesk in one
- 50 free Lyro conversations (lifetime) lowers trial barrier
- Excellent Shopify-specific features (cart preview, order history, discount codes)
- Strong analytics and reporting

**Weaknesses:**
- AI is an add-on, not the core — can feel bolted on
- Pricing gets expensive fast when scaling AI conversations
- Per-conversation billing model is unpredictable for merchants
- Complex product with many features — overwhelming for small stores
- Lyro knowledge training UX can be clunky

### 3. Gorgias (AI Agent / Automate)
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Rating** | 4.3 ⭐ |
| **Reviews** | ~618 |
| **Installs (est.)** | 15,000–25,000 |
| **AI Model** | Proprietary + LLM-powered |
| **Shopify URL** | apps.shopify.com/helpdesk |

**AI Capabilities:**
- AI Agent: fully automated conversation resolution
- Handles returns/refunds, order edits, subscription changes
- Dynamic discount generation
- Product recommendations
- Customizable tone of voice
- Pre- and post-sales FAQ automation
- Available on Email and Chat channels
- Strong feedback loop for AI optimization

**Strengths:**
- Enterprise-grade helpdesk with AI automation on top
- Pay-per-resolution model = only pay for successful automations
- Deep Shopify integration (order actions, not just lookups)
- 150+ integrations (Klaviyo, Recharge, Loop Returns, etc.)
- Strong brand in DTC/e-commerce

**Weaknesses:**
- Primarily a helpdesk — AI chatbot is secondary
- Expensive base plans ($300+/mo for Pro)
- $0.90–$1.00 per AI resolution adds up
- Lower app store rating (4.3) suggests UX friction
- Overkill for small/medium stores wanting just AI chat
- Complex setup

## Key Takeaways for Chatty

1. **The market has cleared out** — Gobot, Relish AI, BotStar, Heyday are all gone. Fewer competitors than expected.
2. **Tidio is the dominant incumbent** but is expensive and complex. Clear opportunity to undercut on simplicity + price.
3. **Zipchat and Juphy are the rising AI-native threats** — purpose-built, high ratings, strong product-market fit.
4. **Gorgias targets enterprise** — not competing at SMB level for pure AI chatbot.
5. **GPT-powered is the standard** — merchants expect ChatGPT-level quality. Proprietary models need to match or exceed.
6. **Self-training (auto-learn from store)** is now table stakes — manual FAQ setup is outdated.
7. **Gap in market**: affordable AI chatbot with strong Shopify integration + multi-channel + easy setup. Chatty could fill this.
