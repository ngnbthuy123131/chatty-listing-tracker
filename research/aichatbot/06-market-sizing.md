## 3. Growth Rate Analysis

### Year-over-Year Growth Indicators

| Metric | 2023→2024 | 2024→2025 | 2025→2026 (proj.) |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-------------------|
| **New AI chatbot apps on Shopify** | +200% (explosion) | +50% (maturing) | +20% (consolidating) |
| **Total AI chatbot installs** | +300% (from low base) | +80-100% | +50-60% |
| **Revenue per install** | Flat / declining (free tiers) | +20% (upselling AI) | +15% (usage-based growth) |
| **Market revenue** | ~150% | ~70-90% | ~60-80% |

### Growth Drivers
1. **Shopify merchant awareness** — AI chatbot category barely existed pre-2023; now Shopify promotes it
2. **Decreasing AI costs** — GPT-4o mini, Claude Haiku make per-conversation cost viable for small stores
3. **Proven ROI** — Early adopters reporting 15-30% reduction in support tickets, 5-15% increase in conversion
4. **Shopify platform support** — Native AI features, better app APIs for order/cart access
5. **WhatsApp/Instagram expansion** — Omnichannel AI drives higher engagement

### Growth Inhibitors
1. **Shopify's own AI** — Shopify Sidekick and potential native AI chat could commoditize
2. **Price sensitivity** — Many Shopify merchants are small businesses; $49+/mo is significant
3. **AI accuracy concerns** — Hallucination risk in product recommendations
4. **Free tier cannibalization** — Apps like Lookfor with generous free plans compress market revenue

## 4. Pricing Trends

### Pricing Model Evolution

```
2020-2022: Flat monthly subscription ($19-$99/mo)
2023:      Flat + AI conversation packs as add-ons
2024:      Usage-based (per AI conversation) becomes standard
2025:      Hybrid: base platform fee + per-conversation/resolution
2026:      Moving toward pay-per-resolution (outcome-based)
```

### Pricing Model Breakdown

| Model | % of Apps | Trend | Pros | Cons |
|-------|-----------|-------|------|------|
| **Flat monthly** | ~30% | Declining | Predictable for merchant | Doesn't scale with value |
| **Per-conversation** | ~40% | Growing | Aligns cost with usage | Unpredictable bills |
| **Per-resolution** | ~10% | Emerging | True outcome-based | Hard to define "resolution" |
| **Freemium + usage** | ~20% | Stable | Low barrier to entry | Low conversion to paid |

### Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)

| Segment | Monthly ARPU | Notes |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| **Free tier users** | $0 | 40-60% of installs |
| **Small stores** (<$50K/yr GMV) | $25-49/mo | Basic AI chat |
| **Mid-market** ($50K-$500K GMV) | $49-149/mo | Full AI + integrations |
| **Enterprise** (>$500K GMV) | $200-1,000+/mo | Custom, multi-channel |
| **Blended ARPU** (paid only) | $55-85/mo | Across all paid tiers |
| **Blended ARPU** (all installs) | $20-35/mo | Including free users |

### Key Pricing Observations
1. **Race to the bottom on basic AI chat** — GPT API costs dropped 90%+ since 2023; basic Q&A becoming cheap
2. **Premium on agentic capabilities** — Apps charging more for AI that takes actions (not just answers)
3. **Bundling trend** — AI chatbot bundled with livechat, helpdesk, email = higher ARPU (Tidio, Gorgias model)
4. **Free tier is essential** — Nearly all successful apps offer free/trial; conversion rate ~15-25% to paid
5. **Per-conversation pricing creates transparency** — Merchants can calculate ROI per interaction

## 5. Opportunity for Chatty (chatty.net)

### Market Entry Assessment

| Factor | Rating | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Market size** | ★★★★★ | $13-29M now, $80-150M by 2028 |
| **Growth rate** | ★★★★★ | 60-90% YoY |
| **Competition intensity** | ★★★★☆ | Crowded but no dominant winner in AI-native space |
| **Barrier to entry** | ★★★☆☆ | AI is commoditizing, but Shopify integration depth matters |
| **Pricing pressure** | ★★★★☆ | Free tiers and falling AI costs compress margins |

### White Space Opportunities
1. **AI chatbot + livechat in one** — Chatty already has livechat; adding AI agent is natural extension (Tidio's playbook)
2. **SMB-focused pricing** — Most AI chatbots price for mid-market; opportunity in $15-29/mo tier for small stores
3. **Shopify-native depth** — Many AI chatbots are multi-platform; going deep on Shopify (order actions, discount creation, checkout integration) is differentiator
4. **Existing install base leverage** — If Chatty has livechat users, upselling AI is 10x easier than cold acquisition
