# Step 4 — Competitor Pricing Analysis

> Last updated: 2026-02-23 | Focus: AI chatbot pricing for Shopify apps

---

## Pricing Model Types in Market

| Model | Description | Who Uses It |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| **Per-conversation** | Charge per AI conversation initiated | Tidio (Lyro) |
| **Per-resolution** | Charge only when AI fully resolves without human | Gorgias |
| **Flat monthly** | Fixed price per tier with conversation limits | Zipchat, Juphy |
| **Usage-based hybrid** | Base plan + overage charges | Tidio, Gorgias |
| **Free tier** | Limited free AI conversations | Tidio, Juphy, AskShop.ai |

|------|---------|----------------------|
| Free | $0 | 50/mo |
| Starter | $24.17/mo | 100/mo |
| Growth | $49.17/mo+ | 250–2,000/mo |
| Plus | $749/mo+ | Custom |
| Premium | Contact sales | Custom |

**Lyro AI Add-on Pricing:**
| AI Conversations/mo | Monthly Price |
|---------------------|--------------|
| 50 (one-time free) | $0 (lifetime, included once) |
| 50/mo | $32.50/mo |
| 100/mo | ~$55/mo |
| 200/mo | ~$95/mo |
| 300/mo | ~$130/mo |
| 500/mo | ~$200/mo |
| 1,000/mo | ~$375/mo |

**Effective cost per AI conversation:** $0.38–$0.65 depending on tier

**Free AI limit:** 50 conversations lifetime (one-time, not recurring)

**Key pricing notes:**
- Premium tier offers pay-per-resolution billing + guaranteed 50% resolution rate
- Can buy Lyro standalone (without helpdesk) starting $32.50/mo
- Total cost = base plan + Lyro add-on + Flows add-on (if wanted)

### Zipchat AI

**Plans (all flat monthly):**
| Plan | Monthly Price | AI Replies/mo | Pages Trained |
|------|--------------|---------------|---------------|
| Starter | $49/mo | 500 | 1,000 |
| Growth | $129/mo | 1,500 | 5,000 |
| Pro | $249/mo | 3,000 | 15,000 |
| Scale | $499/mo | 6,000 | 30,000 |

**Effective cost per AI reply:** $0.08–$0.10

**Free AI limit:** None — no free plan. 7-day free trial only.

**Key pricing notes:**
- Measured in "AI replies" not conversations (a conversation may have multiple replies)
- Very competitive per-reply pricing
- Includes all channels (WhatsApp, Instagram, Email) at every tier
- Self-training included — no extra charge for knowledge base
- Overage pricing unclear (likely throttled or upsold)

|------|--------------|-----------|---------------|
| Starter | $10/mo | 50 | $1.00/resolution |
| Basic | $60/mo | 300 | $0.90/resolution |
| Pro | $360/mo | 2,000 | $0.90/resolution |
| Advanced | $900/mo | 5,000 | $0.90/resolution |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Custom |

**Effective cost per AI resolution:** $0.90–$1.00

**Free AI limit:** None — pay per resolution from first conversation

**Key pricing notes:**
- AI Agent is usage-based on top of base plan — costs are additive
- Only charged for FULLY automated resolutions (not partial)

- Best for stores already using Gorgias for support

## Pricing Model Analysis

### What's Working
- **Flat monthly with clear limits** (Zipchat, Juphy) — merchants prefer predictable costs
- **Free tier** (Tidio, Juphy, AskShop) — critical for adoption; lets merchants try before committing
- **Self-serve pricing** — no "contact sales" barrier for SMBs

### What's NOT Working
- **Per-resolution** (Gorgias) — unpredictable costs, hard to budget
- **Complex add-on stacking** (Tidio) — base + Lyro + Flows = confusing total
- **No free plan** (Zipchat) — higher barrier to entry despite good product

## Pricing Opportunity for Chatty

### Recommended Positioning
| Tier | Suggested Price | AI Conversations | Rationale |
|------|----------------|-------------------|-----------|
| **Free** | $0 | 100/mo | More generous than Tidio (50 lifetime) and Juphy (~50/mo) |
| **Starter** | $29/mo | 500/mo | Undercut Zipchat ($49) with similar value |
| **Growth** | $79/mo | 2,000/mo | Match Juphy, beat Tidio on value |
| **Pro** | $199/mo | 10,000/mo | Clear enterprise path |

**Key pricing strategies:**
1. **Generous free tier** — 100 free AI conversations/mo is a strong differentiator
2. **Flat monthly pricing** — no per-conversation surprises
3. **Include all channels** at every tier (like Zipchat, unlike Tidio)
4. **No add-on complexity** — one price, all features included
5. **Target the $29–$79 sweet spot** where Tidio is overpriced and Zipchat has no free option
