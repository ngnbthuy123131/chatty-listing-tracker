### 6.2 All-in-One Platform Premium

Chatty's unique opportunity: merchants currently buy 2–3 separate tools. An all-in-one captures:

| Scenario | Tools Replaced | Monthly Spend Captured |
|----------|---------------|----------------------|
| Chat only (current) | Shopify Inbox alternative | $20–50/mo |
| Chat + AI | Tidio/Zipchat alternative | $40–100/mo |
| Chat + AI + Helpdesk | Gorgias alternative | $100–400/mo |
| **All-in-one premium** | Full CX stack | **$50–200/mo** (at SMB pricing) |

**The all-in-one premium:** Merchants pay ~20–30% more for a single unified tool than for the cheapest option in one category, but ~50–70% less than buying best-of-breed in each category. This is Chatty's sweet spot.

### 6.4 Key Assumptions & Risks

**Upside drivers:**
- Chatty's existing 50K–120K install base provides warm upsell funnel
- AI costs continuing to drop (GPT-4o mini, Claude Haiku)
- Gorgias moving upmarket, leaving SMB gap
- tawk.to's broken Shopify experience = free-tier opportunity
- Shopify merchant base growing ~10–15%/yr

**Downside risks:**
- Shopify Inbox adds AI → third-party livechat market contracts 30–50%
- Klaviyo enters CX → commoditizes basic helpdesk
- AI deflection reduces ticket volumes → shrinks helpdesk TAM
- Gorgias's Shopify investment creates unfair competitive advantage
- Price sensitivity of Shopify SMBs limits ARPU growth
