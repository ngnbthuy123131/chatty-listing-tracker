# Chatty vs VanChat — Competitive Comparison
*Updated: February 9, 2026*

---

## Executive Summary

**Chatty** and **VanChat** are both Shopify-native AI chatbot apps with "Built for Shopify" badges and sales-first positioning. However, they operate at fundamentally different scales: Chatty has **27x more installs** (9,539 vs 351), **15x more reviews** (1,741 vs 112), and **~2-3x more revenue** ($444K ARR vs ~$120-240K est.). Chatty is a growth-stage product within a profitable 250-person parent company (Avada Commerce), while VanChat is an early-stage startup with ~10 people and seed funding.

VanChat recently **restructured its pricing dramatically** — dropping from credit-based plans ($39-$599) to visitor-based plans ($19-$99) with unlimited replies. This directly challenges Chatty's price advantage and removes VanChat's #1 user complaint (credit limits). Despite smaller scale, VanChat claims **97% AI accuracy** using dual AI models (GPT-4o + Claude 3) and offers features Chatty lacks, including add-to-cart in chat and a more aggressive proactive sales engine.

**Bottom line**: Chatty wins on scale, trust, and ecosystem. VanChat wins on AI sophistication and (now) pricing simplicity. VanChat's pricing pivot is the most important competitive signal — it's a direct attack on Chatty's value positioning.

## 3. Pricing Comparison

### Chatty Pricing (unchanged)

| Plan | Price/mo | AI Conversations | Products | Team Members |
|------|----------|-----------------|----------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | 50 lifetime | 100 | 1 |
| Basic | $19.99 | 50/month | 500 | 5 |
| Pro | $68.99 | 300/month | 8,000 | 10 |
| Plus | $199 | 700/month | 20,000 | Unlimited |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Custom | Custom |

Overage: **$0.40/conversation**. Annual discount available (~15% off).

### VanChat Pricing (NEW — restructured in late 2025/early 2026)

| Plan | Price/mo | Visitors | Products | Chat Replies |
|------|----------|----------|----------|--------------|
| Free | $0 | Dev stores only | All features | All features |
| Starter | $19 | 100/month | **Unlimited** | **Unlimited** |
| Advanced | $49 | 1,000/month | **Unlimited** | **Unlimited** |
| Growth | $99 | 10,000/month | **Unlimited** | **Unlimited** |

**Previous pricing** (before restructure): Startup $39, Pro $99-149, Growth $249, Scale $599 — credit-based with GPT reply limits.

### Pricing Analysis

| Comparison Point | Chatty | VanChat | Advantage |
|-----------------|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Cheapest paid plan** | $19.99/mo | $19/mo | VanChat (barely) |
| **Billing model** | Conversation-based (flat count + overage) | **Visitor-based (unlimited replies)** | VanChat |
| **Mid-tier price** | $68.99/mo (Pro) | $49/mo (Advanced) | VanChat |
| **High-tier price** | $199/mo (Plus) | $99/mo (Growth) | VanChat (2x cheaper) |
| **Overage costs** | $0.40/conversation | None (visitor limits) | VanChat |
| **Annual discount** | ~15% off | ~17% off | Similar |
| **Free plan** | 50 lifetime conversations | Dev stores only | Chatty |

**This is a major strategic shift.** VanChat's new pricing:
1. **Eliminates credit anxiety** — the #1 user complaint about VanChat was credit-based limits. Now replies are unlimited.
2. **Undercuts Chatty at every tier** — especially at mid/high tiers ($49 vs $68.99; $99 vs $199).
3. **Shifts to visitor-based billing** — simpler to understand, but may limit value for high-traffic stores with low chat engagement.
4. **Removes the Scale plan ($599)** — suggests VanChat is focusing on SMB, not enterprise.

**Chatty's remaining pricing advantages:**
- Free plan is **genuinely usable** (50 conversations) vs VanChat's dev-store-only free tier
- Flat-rate pricing with known overage costs ($0.40) is predictable
- Enterprise tier exists for larger merchants

## 10. Strategic Assessment

### Where Chatty Wins vs VanChat

1. **Scale and trust** — 1,741 reviews create a powerful Shopify App Store flywheel. Merchants trust Chatty based on volume of social proof.
2. **Ecosystem** — Avada's 300K merchants provide a distribution channel VanChat can never match.
3. **Helpdesk features** — Unified inbox, team collaboration, CSAT, FAQ builder — Chatty is a more complete support solution.
4. **Profitability** — Chatty is profitable and self-sustaining. VanChat is burning cash.
5. **Mobile app** — Chatty has iOS/Android apps; VanChat does not.

### Where VanChat Wins vs Chatty

1. **AI quality** — Dual AI model (GPT-4o + Claude 3) and 97% accuracy claims are consistently backed by user reviews praising "no hallucination." Chatty's single-model approach has more accuracy complaints.
2. **Sales engine** — Add-to-cart in chat, proactive discount delivery, and aggressive revenue attribution make VanChat a stronger *sales* tool.
3. **New pricing** — Unlimited replies at $19-$99/mo eliminates credit anxiety and undercuts Chatty at every tier above Basic.
4. **Case study ROI** — VanChat's 10,000-58,000% ROI numbers are more compelling than Chatty's absolute-dollar case studies.
5. **Brand voice customization** — Deeper tone/personality controls.

### What Chatty Should Do About VanChat

| Priority | Action | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| **P0** | Monitor VanChat's install trajectory post-pricing change | If installs reverse the decline, the new pricing is working |
| **P1** | Add **add-to-cart in chat** | Table-stakes feature that VanChat, Manifest AI, and Rep AI all have |
| **P1** | Improve **revenue attribution dashboard** | VanChat's ROI dashboard is a selling point; Chatty's analytics are the #1 complaint |
| **P2** | Consider **dual AI model** support | GPT-4o + Claude 3 dual-model approach could improve accuracy |
| **P2** | Strengthen proactive sales features | VanChat's discount delivery and browse-triggered pop-ups are more aggressive |
| **P3** | Consider visitor-based pricing option | VanChat's move to "unlimited replies" resonates with merchants who fear overage costs |

### What VanChat Should Do About Chatty

| Priority | Action | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| **P0** | Grow install base past 500+ | At 351 and declining, growth is existential |
| **P1** | Build social media presence | Zero social = leaving growth on the table |
| **P1** | Add FAQ builder / help center | Chatty's FAQ builder is a unique differentiator |
| **P2** | Build mobile app | Table-stakes for live chat agents |
| **P2** | Develop Klaviyo integration | Key for Shopify merchants; Chatty already has it |
| **P3** | Raise Series A | Need capital to compete with Chatty's Avada ecosystem scale |

## 11. Verdict

| Dimension | Winner | Why |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| **For solo operators / tiny stores** | **Chatty** | Free plan with 50 conversations + FAQ builder |
| **For sales-focused merchants** | **VanChat** | Better AI, add-to-cart, proactive sales, ROI tracking |
| **For support-focused merchants** | **Chatty** | Unified inbox, team features, CSAT, mobile app |
| **For price-sensitive merchants** | **VanChat** | Unlimited replies at every tier; no overage costs |
| **For mid-market / growing stores** | **Tie** | VanChat cheaper but Chatty more complete |
| **For enterprise** | **Chatty** | Enterprise tier exists; VanChat removed their Scale plan |
| **For long-term reliability** | **Chatty** | Profitable, 8-year parent company, 300K ecosystem |
| **Overall** | **Chatty** | Scale, trust, profitability > AI sophistication at this stage |

**Chatty is the safer, more complete choice. VanChat is the more innovative, riskier bet.**

Chatty should not be complacent — VanChat's pricing restructure and AI quality are real threats. But VanChat's declining installs and tiny team make it unlikely to overtake Chatty in the near term. The real competitive threat to both remains Tidio ($48M ARR, 300K+ businesses) and Shopify's native tools.
