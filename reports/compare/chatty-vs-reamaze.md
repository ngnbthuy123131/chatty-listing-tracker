## Executive Summary

**Re:amaze is a mature eCommerce helpdesk; Chatty is a sales-first AI chatbot built for Shopify.** Re:amaze (acquired by BigCommerce in 2022) excels at multi-channel support consolidation — email, chat, social, SMS, voice — with strong helpdesk workflows for support teams. But its chatbot is rule-based, its AI is agent-assist (not autonomous), and it has no revenue attribution or proactive sales engine. Chatty is the opposite: lightweight on classical helpdesk features, but its GPT-4 AI sells autonomously 24/7, proactively engages shoppers, and directly ties conversations to revenue. **For Shopify merchants who primarily need a support inbox and ticketing system, Re:amaze is the stronger tool. For merchants who want AI to actively drive sales, Chatty wins clearly.**

## When Re:amaze Makes Sense

- You need robust email helpdesk across multiple inboxes and brands
- You handle high-volume support tickets and need workflow automations
- Your team uses SMS and voice support
- You're on WooCommerce, BigCommerce, or Magento (not Shopify-exclusive)
- You need staff scheduling, shift management, and detailed agent performance reporting
- You want live screensharing to help customers in real-time

## When Chatty Makes Sense

- You want AI to sell proactively — not just respond to support questions
- You want to attribute chat directly to revenue
- You're on Shopify and want the deepest native integration
- You want autonomous AI handling conversations 24/7 (no manual bot flows to build)
- You prefer flat-rate pricing over per-agent billing
- You need AI product recommendations, cart recovery, and size guide intelligence

## Strategic Implications for Chatty

### Where Chatty Wins

1. **AI quality** — Re:amaze's bots are rule-based: you build flow trees of if/then logic. Chatty's AI understands natural language, reads product catalogs, answers novel questions, and sells. There's no comparison in intelligence or maintenance burden.

2. **Sales-first** — Re:amaze is a support consolidation tool. Chatty is an AI salesperson. Revenue attribution, proactive triggers, and cart recovery are core to Chatty and absent in Re:amaze.

3. **Shopify depth** — Re:amaze supports 4+ platforms, which means its Shopify integration is good but not maximized. Chatty is Shopify-only, earning the "Built for Shopify" badge and deeper platform trust.

4. **Pricing at scale** — A 5-person team on Re:amaze Plus pays $345/mo. Chatty Plus is $199/mo for unlimited agents. As teams grow, Chatty's economics improve dramatically.

5. **Shopify App Store** — Chatty has 4.9★ with 1,735+ reviews vs Re:amaze's 4.7★ with ~350 reviews. When merchants discover tools through the App Store, Chatty is the obvious choice by social proof.

### Where Chatty Needs Work

1. **Email helpdesk** — Re:amaze's unlimited email inboxes, department routing, and ticket workflow automation are genuinely superior for teams handling large support volumes. Chatty's email is limited and basic.

2. **Multi-platform** — Re:amaze supports WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and Magento. This lets them serve a broader market. Chatty is Shopify-only.

3. **SMS/Voice** — Re:amaze Pro adds SMS and voice channels, appealing to merchants with older customer demographics who prefer non-chat support.

4. **Advanced agent tools** — Screensharing, video calls, staff shifts, and detailed performance reporting are enterprise-grade agent features where Re:amaze leads.

### Positioning Opportunity

**"Re:amaze manages support. Chatty generates revenue."** The core message: Re:amaze is a better helpdesk, but if your goal is to grow sales — not just handle tickets — Chatty's proactive AI, revenue attribution, and autonomous selling create measurable ROI that Re:amaze simply cannot deliver. Frame the comparison as a different category, not just feature differences.
