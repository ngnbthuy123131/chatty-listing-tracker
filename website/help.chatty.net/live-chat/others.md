## Availability

This feature is available for all users.

## Resolve Conversations

Marking conversations as finished moves them from your "Open" list to "Resolved." This capability assists with:

- Maintaining inbox organization
- Identifying customers requiring assistance
- Documenting completed interactions

### Manual Resolution

1. Navigate to the conversation in your Inbox
2. Select the Resolve button within the chat
3. The conversation transfers to your Resolved folder

### Automatic Resolution

**How it functions:**
- Older conversations lacking recent messages get automatically resolved
- A notice message explains this action to customers
- Customers can restart the chat by sending another message

**Setup process:**

1. Access Settings
2. Navigate to Automation
3. Enable Automatic resolution
4. Configure preferences:
   - **Auto resolve after**: Duration to wait
   - **Time unit**: Minutes, hours, or days
   - **Notice message**: Customer-facing explanation
5. Save changes

**Recommended timeframes:**
- High-volume businesses: 2-4 hours
- Specialized support: 24 hours
- Round-the-clock operations: 4-8 hours

**Best practices:**
- Don't resolve while awaiting customer response
- Ask "Anything else I can help with?" before resolving

**Notification tips:**
- Use friendly language
- Communicate ease of reopening
- Example: "This chat was closed due to no activity. Send a message to reopen it - we're here to help!"

## Transcript (Copies of Conversation)

A conversation transcript is an email copy of the complete chat conversation, including all messages, timestamps, and customer information.

### Why Customers Need Transcripts
- Reference product suggestions later
- Share technical guidance with others
- Document service interactions

### Why Teams Need Transcripts
- Escalate issues across departments
- Maintain compliance and training records
- Share context with uninvolved team members

### Enable Transcript Feature

1. Go to Settings
2. Select Channels
3. Click Email channel
4. Access Preferences
5. Turn on Conversation transcript email
6. In "Forward to," input recipient email addresses (separated by commas if multiple)
7. Optional: Configure Email signature
   - Toggle Email signature on
   - Enter signature details
8. Save

### Customer Request Process

Customers can request transcripts during conversations:

1. Click the menu icon (three dots) in the chat's top right
2. Select "Email transcript"
3. Transcript automatically sends to their email

### Team Member Sending Process

1. Open the conversation
2. Click three dots (top right) → "Send transcript"
3. Select recipient:
   - Your Chatty account email
   - Customer's email address
   - Custom email address
4. Click Done

## Satisfaction Survey

This feature helps you gather feedback from customers after conversations are resolved.

### Benefits
- Monitor support team performance trends
- Recognize conversations needing improvement
- Apply insights for enhanced satisfaction
- Demonstrate customer care commitment

### Configuration Steps

**Step 1:** Navigate to Settings → Automation → Satisfaction survey → Manage

**Step 2:** Activate the Survey toggle

**Step 3:** Choose rating method:
- **Star rating**: 1-5 star system
- **Emoji scale**: Five emoji faces ranging from very unhappy to very happy

**Step 4:** Configure content:
- **Intro question**: Customer's initial prompt (Default: "How was your experience?")
- **Thank you message**: Post-submission response (Default: "Thank you for your feedback!")

**Step 5:** Select trigger timing:
- **Conversation is resolved**: Survey displays upon manual resolution
- **Specific keywords appear**: Trigger activates when customers mention particular words (examples: "thank you," "thanks," "got it," "perfect")

**Step 6:** Save to activate
