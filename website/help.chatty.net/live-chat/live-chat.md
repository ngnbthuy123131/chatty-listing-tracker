# Live Chat

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/live-chat/live-chat

## Availability

This feature is available for all users.

## Overview

Live chat enables customers to contact you during their store visits, allowing immediate responses and efficient chat management.

## Purpose

The feature facilitates real-time customer communication and helps organize conversations through an inbox system.

## Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Enable Chatbox

Navigate to Chatbox settings and activate it in the General section.

### Step 2: Activate Live Chat

In the Blocks section, turn on the live chat feature.

### Step 3: Configure Pre-Chat Form

Click Edit to customize the information collection process. Options include:
- Required information fields (email mandatory; name and phone optional)
- Anonymous chat permission (allows users to chat without providing details)

**Tip:** Preview the form by clicking "Chat now" in the preview area.

### Step 4: Save Changes

Click the Save button to apply your settings.

### Step 5: Test on Store

Visit your storefront to verify the chatbox appearance, then initiate a test conversation.

## Additional Configuration

You can establish online/offline status through the online hours settings, allowing you to control when chat availability is displayed.
