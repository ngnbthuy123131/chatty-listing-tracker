# Inbox

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/live-chat/inbox

## Availability

This feature is available for all users.

## What is Inbox

Inbox is your central hub for managing all customer conversations across multiple channels. It consolidates messages from live chat, email, Facebook Messenger, and other integrated platforms into a unified workspace, enabling you to monitor, reply to, and systematize every customer engagement.

## What Inbox Helps You Do

- Respond to all conversations from all channels in one place
- Easily find conversation with smart filter & search
- Check customer details while chatting
- Manage conversations effectively: star, mark as resolved, etc.

## How to Use Inbox

### Access Your Inbox

1. Go to Chatty in your Shopify admin
2. Click **Inbox** tab in the main navigation
3. Or click **Go to inbox** from Dashboard

Your inbox opens with the conversation list on the left and chat details on the right.

### Respond to Conversations

**Send messages:**

1. Select a conversation from the list
2. Click the message input field at the bottom
3. Type your message
4. Press **Enter** or click **Send**

> With conversation assigned to AI, click **Join conversations** to start responding.

**Change send behavior:**

By default, pressing **Enter** sends your message. You can modify this preference in **Settings** → **Inbox settings** → **Message preference**.

### Filter Conversations

**Filter by status:**

1. Click filter options in the filter menu or at the top of conversation list
2. Select **Open**, **Resolved**, **Starred**, **Blocked**, or **Unread**
3. Conversation list updates to show only matching conversations

**Filter by channel:**

1. Click channel icons in the filter menu
2. Select specific channel (**Online store**, **Live chat**, **Email**, **Messenger**, **Instagram**, **WhatsApp**)
3. View only conversations from that channel

**Filter by assignee:**

1. Select **Your inbox** (conversations assigned to you) or **Unassigned** from the filter menu; alternatively, select an assignee in the list at the top of conversation list
2. View conversations assigned to that assignee

### Manage Conversations

**Mark as resolved:**

1. Open the conversation
2. Handle the customer inquiry completely
3. Click **Resolve**
4. Conversation moves to **Resolved** filter

**Star conversations:**

1. Open important conversation
2. Click star icon
3. Find starred conversations in Starred filter

**Delete conversations:**

1. Open conversation to delete
2. Click **Delete**

> Deleting conversations removes contact info of anonymous users and leads, while customer contact information remains unchanged. Deleting contacts will also remove all their conversations.

### Check Customer Details

Access important customer information during conversations:

- Contact information (email, phone)
- Order history and purchase details
- Customer tags and notes
- Pages they've browsed on your store
- Real-time actions:
  - View FAQs
  - Add products to empty cart (with product details)
  - Add products to existing cart (with product details)
  - Remove products from cart (with product details)
  - Place an order

### Use with Team Members

When working with team members:

1. **Assign conversations**: Select team member in conversation details
2. **Add notes**: Leave context for teammates handling the conversation
3. **Track assignments**: Filter by assigned conversations
4. **Collaborate**: Multiple team members can view conversation history
