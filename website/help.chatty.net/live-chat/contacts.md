# Contacts

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/live-chat/contacts

## Availability

This feature is available for all users.

## Overview

The Contacts feature enables comprehensive customer management, including accessing conversation histories, reviewing behavioral data, modifying contact information, removing contacts, and extracting contact datasets.

## Key Capabilities

- Retrieve customer details encompassing chat records and behavioral insights
- Modify contact information
- Remove contacts from the system
- Download contact data

**Important:** If you delete a contact, all conversations of that contact will be deleted too.

## Customer Classification

The system recognizes three customer categories:

- **Guest**: Individuals who provide email addresses to initiate conversations or interact through integrated channels (Email, Messenger, Instagram)
- **Customer**: Users who have made purchases or subscribed to the store
- **Anonymous User**: Individuals chatting without identifying themselves

## Contact Information Available

Users can access comprehensive contact data:
- Conversation records
- Activity chronology
- Summary metrics (purchase count, lifetime value, registration date)
- Personal information (name, contact details, location)
- Additional fields (labels, internal notes)

The system allows modification of profiles, labels, and notes, plus direct navigation to Shopify customer profiles.

## Contact List Management

The contacts interface displays total contact count, provides search functionality, and enables filtering by customer classification.

## Contact Export Feature

### Exportable Data Elements
- Customer classification type
- Personal contact details
- Purchase metrics
- Interaction timeline data
- Labels and internal notes

### Export Process
1. Navigate to Contacts section
2. Choose scope (current page or all contacts)
3. Initiate export function
4. Access downloaded file from local storage
