# Quick Replies

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/live-chat/quick-replies

## Availability

This feature is accessible to all users.

## Overview

Quick replies help users set up messages which are common for merchants to answer customers.

## Creating Quick Replies

1. Navigate to **Settings** and locate the Quick replies section, then select **Manage**
2. Enable the feature by toggling on Quick replies in your chat settings
3. Click the **Create quick reply** button
4. Complete the form by entering:
   - A shortcut (the trigger text)
   - A category (create new ones as needed by typing and selecting Add)
   - Your response message
5. Click **Save** to store your template
6. Manage existing replies using the edit and delete options available in your list

## Using Quick Replies During Conversations

Access your saved templates while chatting through these methods:

- Click the quick replies icon or type `/` to display available options
- Select your desired reply from the list to insert it

Additional chat options include:
- Click the add icon to create new replies directly from conversation view
- Click the settings icon to access quick replies management
