# Team Member Guide

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/live-chat/team/team-member

## Availability

This feature is available for Basic and Pro plan users.

## Overview

Team members invited to Chatty collaborate to manage conversations efficiently. Access the web app at https://app.meetchatty.com/.

## Team Member Permissions

Collaborative features include:

- Responding to conversations across all channels (Live chat, Email, Facebook Messenger, etc.)
- Accessing conversation histories and customer profiles
- Creating internal notes
- Resolving, marking as unread, and blocking conversations
- Assigning conversations to teammates
- Configuring notifications and updating personal profiles

> Permissions and settings are managed by the admin.

Contact your administrator regarding role or permission changes.

## Getting Started Steps

1. **Accept Invitation** - Check your email for the Chatty invitation and select "Create account"
2. **Create Account** - Set a password, then access Settings to edit your name and profile picture
3. **Understand Interface** - The main workspace is the Inbox, displaying conversations and account settings

## Key Features

**Notification Setup:** Navigate to Settings to configure device notifications for incoming messages.

**Online/Offline Status:** Set your availability status when logging into Chatty through the status indicator.

**Multi-Store Management:** Switch between different stores (linked to your account) to manage conversations from multiple locations.

**Team Communication:** You and your team can leave private comments within conversations by Internal note. Mention colleagues using the Internal note feature for private team collaboration.
