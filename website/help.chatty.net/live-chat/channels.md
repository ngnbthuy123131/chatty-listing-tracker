# Channels

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/live-chat/channels

## Availability

This feature is accessible to all Chatty users.

## Overview

Chatty enables centralized management of conversations across multiple communication channels. The platform consolidates messages from email, Facebook Messenger, Instagram, and live chat into a single inbox interface.

## Email Channel

### Key Features

The email integration allows users to receive customer emails directly within Chatty. You can only connect one email to Chatty. Emails appear in the Inbox with a designated email icon indicator.

### Configuration Options

Users can establish three email settings:
- **Email forwarding**: The email address connected to Chatty where inboxes are routed
- **Email sender**: The address used when replying to customers through Chatty
- **Email signature**: Custom signature appended to outgoing customer emails

### Conversation History Feature

This optional feature sends the complete chat transcript to customers when conversations are marked resolved. To enable:

1. Navigate to Channels section
2. Select Email channel
3. Access Preferences
4. Activate Conversation history toggle
5. Specify recipient email addresses for history delivery

### Email Sender Configuration

The default sender address is `noreply@chattyemail.com`. To customize:

1. Access Email sender settings
2. Choose "Own email address" option
3. Select "Add and verify email"
4. Input your email address and select Verify
5. Locate verification link in your email inbox
6. Return to app and click Done
7. Assign a name for the email sender

## Facebook Messenger & Instagram

Connecting a Facebook account enables receipt and response to both Facebook and Instagram messages within Chatty.

### Connected Pages Management

After Facebook account connection, users can review all linked pages in the Channels interface.

### Message Identification

Messages display channel-specific icons: Messenger icon for Facebook messages, Instagram icon for Instagram content. Users can also filter by selecting "Messenger" or "Instagram" in the Channel filter.

### Important Note

By disconnecting with Facebook fanpages, existing conversations will stay in Inbox without update.

### Access Permissions

Chatty requires Facebook Page access to: display messages in the app, enable direct replies from Chatty, and organize conversations by associated pages.

## WhatsApp Channel

WhatsApp integration allows message monitoring within Chatty. You can connect multiple WhatsApp accounts to Chatty.

### Prerequisites

Before connecting, ensure you have:
- An active Facebook business page
- A WhatsApp Business account connected to that page
- Administrative permissions for both accounts
