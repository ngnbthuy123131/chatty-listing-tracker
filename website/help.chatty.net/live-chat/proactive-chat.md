# Proactive Chat

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/live-chat/proactive-chat

## Availability

This feature is accessible to all users.

## Overview

Proactive chat sends targeted messages to website visitors before they ask questions. Rather than waiting for customer inquiries, these campaigns strategically engage shoppers at critical purchasing moments.

## Strategic Benefits

1. **Visitor Conversion:** Welcome new guests and direct them toward relevant merchandise, preventing aimless browsing.
2. **Sales Recovery:** Automatically reach customers with abandoned carts, offering incentives or support.
3. **List Growth:** Encourage newsletter subscriptions through exclusive discount offers at optimal timing.
4. **Revenue Enhancement:** Feature bestselling or complementary items when purchase signals appear.

## Available Templates

### Welcome Visitors
- **Goal:** Personalized greetings for new arrivals
- **Ideal for:** First-time visitors, high-traffic areas, brand introduction
- **Sample message:** "Hi there! How can we help you?"
- **Placement:** Homepage, category pages, landing pages

### Newsletter Subscription
- **Goal:** Email capture through exclusive offers
- **Ideal for:** Lead generation, list building, promotional discounts
- **Sample message:** "Don't miss out! Discover the special offers we've tailored for you!"
- **Includes:** Discount codes
- **Placement:** Product pages, exit-intent moments, returning visitors

### Product Recommendation
- **Goal:** Drive sales through personalized suggestions
- **Ideal for:** Cross-selling, bestseller promotion, customized experiences
- **Sample message:** "You might like this product!"
- **Includes:** Product details
- **Placement:** Category pages, product details, checkout

### Cart Booster
- **Goal:** Convert hesitant buyers with incentives
- **Ideal for:** Cart recovery, completion increase, urgency messaging
- **Sample message:** "Good choice! Thanks for your interest! Want to learn more or place an order now?"
- **Includes:** Discounts or product information
- **Placement:** Cart page, checkout abandonment, delayed purchases

### Abandoned Cart Reminder
- **Goal:** Encourage completion of unfinished purchases
- **Ideal for:** Recovery campaigns, abandonment reduction, order completion
- **Sample message:** "You left some items in your cart! Need help deciding?"
- **Placement:** Cart page, post-inactivity (3-5 minutes), post-add scenarios

### Collection Boost
- **Goal:** Enhance engagement on collection browsing
- **Ideal for:** Seasonal campaigns, new launches, cross-selling
- **Sample message:** "New winter arrivals just dropped! Want to see our cozy essentials?"
- **Placement:** Collection pages

## Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Navigate to Proactive Chat
Access the proactive chat section from your dashboard.

### Step 2: Initiate Campaign
Select the "Create proactive chat" option.

### Step 3: Select Campaign Type
Choose from four primary types:
- Welcome visitor messaging
- Newsletter subscription capture
- Product recommendation display
- Cart booster targeting

### Step 4: Configure Campaign Settings

**Campaign Details:**
- Assign an identifying name for dashboard reference
- Toggle activation to enable live storefront display

**Targeting Configuration:**
- **Trigger settings:** Define activation conditions
- **Page selection:** Specify target pages (press Enter after each URL entry)
- **Audience scope:** All visitors or returning customers only
- **Device targeting:** Desktop, mobile, or both
- **Display timing:** Page load moment, scroll percentage, or delay option
- **Visibility duration:** Determine display length

**Message Configuration:**
- **Primary message:** Text displayed above the chat interface
- **Rich content:** Product suggestions (Product recommendations and Cart booster only)
- **Chat response:** Message shown when visitors interact (Subscribe newsletter and Cart booster); discounts included

### Step 5: Launch Campaign
- Use **Preview** to verify storefront appearance
- Click **Activate** to deploy

## Advanced Features

### Campaign Priority System

When multiple campaigns could activate simultaneously, priority determines display order:
- Highest-priority campaigns appear first (ranked 1-highest)
- Multiple campaigns per visitor per session are possible
- Adjust priority in campaign settings

### Double Opt-in (Newsletter Template)

This optional feature requires customers to confirm email subscriptions via confirmation message before receiving discount benefits.

### Timing Recommendations

- Welcome messages: 5-10 seconds post-load
- Product recommendations: 30-50% scroll completion
- Cart boosters: 2-3 minutes after cart addition
- Newsletter signups: Pre-exit or post-browsing multiple products
