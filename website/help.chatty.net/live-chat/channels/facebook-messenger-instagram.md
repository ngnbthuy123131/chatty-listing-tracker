# Facebook Messenger & Instagram Integration

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/live-chat/channels/facebook-messenger-instagram

## Overview

This feature enables users to manage communications from both Facebook Messenger and Instagram direct messages within a single unified inbox interface.

## Key Benefits

- Manage all conversations from Facebook and Instagram in one centralized location
- Reduces time spent switching between platforms
- Enhances customer response speed and consistency
- Enables tracking of customer touchpoints across channels
- Allows responses via customers' preferred communication method

## Connection Requirements

Only Instagram business accounts that are already linked to a Facebook Page can be integrated.

## Setup Instructions

1. Navigate to Settings → Channels
2. Locate Messenger/Instagram and select Manage
3. Click Connect to Facebook
4. Provide Facebook login credentials
5. Choose which pages to connect
6. Save selections

## Account Management Options

**Adding Additional Pages:** Click Reconnect, select Edit previous settings, choose additional pages, and save.

**Removing Pages:** Click the Edit icon on a connected page, toggle it off, and save changes.

**Switching Accounts:** Disconnect the current account, then connect a new Facebook account.

## Important Limitations

Only one Facebook account can connect to Chatty, though multiple pages from that single account are supported.

## Message Handling

Connected messages appear in the inbox with distinct Messenger or Instagram icons for easy identification and filtering.
