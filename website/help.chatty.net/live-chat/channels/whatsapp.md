# WhatsApp Integration

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/live-chat/channels/whatsapp

## Availability

This feature is available to all users.

## Overview

The WhatsApp feature allows users to monitor WhatsApp messages directly within the Chatty platform. Multiple WhatsApp accounts can be linked to Chatty.

## Prerequisites

- An active Facebook business page
- A WhatsApp Business account connected to that Facebook page
- Administrative permissions for both accounts

## Connection Process

### Step 1: Access Channel Settings
Navigate to the Channels section of your Chatty dashboard, locate WhatsApp, and select Manage.

### Step 2: Connect Facebook Account
Click "Connect account" and complete the Facebook authentication process by entering credentials and approving requested permissions.

### Step 3: Select WhatsApp Account
Choose your desired WhatsApp Business account from the list of available accounts linked to your Facebook page.

### Step 4: Confirm Connection
Verify successful connection by checking for the WhatsApp icon in your inbox. Test by sending a message to your WhatsApp Business number.

## Setting Up WhatsApp Business

1. Download the WhatsApp Business app
2. Access Tools tab and select Facebook & Instagram
3. Log into Facebook and select your business page
4. Click "Connect WhatsApp" to establish the link
5. Confirm successful connection when the WhatsApp button appears on your page

## WhatsApp Templates

Templates are pre-approved message formats for initiating customer conversations. They're required when messaging customers who haven't previously messaged you or haven't responded within 24 hours.

### Template Categories

- **Marketing**: Promotional content and announcements
- **Utility**: Order updates and appointment reminders
- **Authentication**: Not supported through this integration

After customer replies, normal conversations are available for 24 hours without template requirements.

## FAQs

**Missing WhatsApp Accounts:** If no accounts appear during connection, verify your WhatsApp Business account is connected to your Facebook page through Facebook settings.

**Personal WhatsApp Limitations:** Only WhatsApp Business accounts integrate with Chatty. Personal accounts cannot be connected.

**Creating New Accounts:** New WhatsApp Business accounts can be created during setup, generating a new business number.

**Pending Number Status:** "Pending" status indicates Meta approval is still processing. Common causes include:
- Two-Factor Authentication not enabled on your WhatsApp Business app
- Display name not meeting Meta guidelines

Enable 2FA in Settings → Account → Two-step verification and allow 24-48 hours for approval.

**Disconnecting WhatsApp:** Users can disconnect WhatsApp anytime through Channels → WhatsApp → Manage. Existing conversations remain accessible, but new message syncing stops.

**Customer Visibility:** Customers perceive responses as coming directly from your WhatsApp Business account; Chatty's involvement remains invisible.
