# Customers

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/live-chat/inbox/customers

## Availability

This feature is available for all users.

## Customer Types

The system categorizes visitors into three distinct groups:

1. **Guest (Lead)** — Site visitors who haven't purchased, shown with "Customer icon + email"
2. **Customer** — Individuals who've made purchases, displayed as "Name in icon + customer name"
3. **Anonymous User** — Those using the "Chat with us as anonymous" option, shown with "Anonymous icon + random name"

## Why Guests Don't Auto-Convert

The platform deliberately avoids automatic conversion to maintain data accuracy and protect your customers' experience. Automatic conversion risks creating multiple profiles for the same customer or generating profiles with incomplete information, and could prevent customers from accessing their accounts.

**Manual alternative:** Review guest information before manually creating validated customer profiles.

## Managing Customers

### Blocking Users

Access a customer's conversation and click "Block user" in the right panel to confirm. Customers who get blocked won't be notified that they're blocked. They can still message you in the chatbox, but they cannot receive replies.

### Deleting Customers

Customer deletion occurs in the Contacts section, a separate management area for customer information.
