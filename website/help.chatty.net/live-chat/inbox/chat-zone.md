# Chat Zone

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/live-chat/inbox/chat-zone

## Availability

This feature is currently in beta and available only to select users.

## Overview

The chat zone is where you engage and communicate with your customers.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Shortcut | Function |
|----------|----------|
| `/` | Open quick replies |
| `?` | Insert FAQs |
| `%` | Insert discounts |
| `!` | Insert products |
| `Ctrl + B` | Bold text |
| `Ctrl + I` | Italic text |
| `@` (in Note) | Tag team member |

## Customer Online Status

The top banner displays whether your customer is currently online or offline. When a customer is offline, messages are automatically sent to their email. For anonymous users without email addresses, you must provide their email to continue the conversation.

## Customer Behavior Tracking

The chat zone displays customer actions including:
- Starting a chat
- Adding products to empty cart (with product details shown)
- Adding products to existing cart (with product details shown)
- Removing products from cart (with product details shown)
- Placing an order

## Sending Messages

Type your message and press **Send** or **Enter** to transmit it. The **Enter** key behavior is customizable through Settings → General → Inbox Settings → Select method to send message.

## Text Formatting

Messages support formatting options:

**Bold and Italic**: Use the **B** and *I* buttons or press `Ctrl + B` and `Ctrl + I` respectively.

**Links**: Add links in three ways:
- Click the hyperlink icon and enter your text and URL
- Type text, select it, click the hyperlink icon, and enter your URL
- Type a URL and click **Edit** to format it

## Emoji

Send emojis by clicking the emoji icon in the chat interface.

## Message Reactions

You can react to messages with emojis or reply directly to specific messages in the conversation thread.

## Insert Discounts

1. Click the discount icon in conversation
2. Select your desired discount from available options
3. Click **Send**
4. The discount appears with a "Shop now" button allowing customers to purchase with the applied discount

## Insert FAQs

Click the FAQ icon to access your FAQ hub. Quickly select and insert answers directly into the chat. Note: FAQs can only be added in your default language.

## Send Files

1. Click the file attachment icon
2. Select up to 10 files per upload (unlimited total)
3. Click **Send**

Supported image formats are JPG, JPEG, PNG, and GIF with a maximum file size of 20MB per file.

## Quick Replies

Access quick replies by clicking the quick replies icon or typing `/`. Select your preset responses to send quickly. Configure quick replies in Settings → Quick Replies.

## Insert Products

1. Click the product icon
2. Select a product variant or collection
3. Click **Insert**

Products appear in chat with images and details, allowing customers to view and purchase directly.
