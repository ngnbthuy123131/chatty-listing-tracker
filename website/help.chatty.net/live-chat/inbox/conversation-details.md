# Conversation Details

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/live-chat/inbox/conversation-details

## Availability

This feature is available for all users.

## Overview

The conversation details section allows you to access comprehensive customer information including:

- Contact information (email, phone, etc.)
- Order details
- Notes
- Checklist
- Shopping cart
- Browsed pages

## Customer Behavior Tracking

Real-time monitoring of customer actions displays in the chat zone, showing activities such as:

- Initiating a chat session
- Adding products to an empty cart (with product details)
- Adding products to an existing cart (with product details)
- Removing products from cart (with product details)
- Placing an order

## Notes Management

Users can create notes for each customer to store additional information. Each note includes a timestamp and can be edited or deleted as needed.

## Customer Types and Associated Information

### Guest Customers

Limited visibility restricted to the email address entered in the live chat form. An "Add customer" option enables creation of new customer profiles within Shopify.

### Registered Customers

Customers with purchase history display synced Shopify data including name, email, phone, location, and tags. This information appears both in detailed profiles and beneath the customer name.

### Anonymous Users

Even without identification data, anonymous visitors can be added as new customers using the "Add customer" button.
