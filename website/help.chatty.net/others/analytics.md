# Analytics

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/others/analytics

## Overview

Analytics delivers comprehensive insights into your customer conversations, AI assistant performance, sales contribution, and FAQs engagement. This tool is accessible to all users.

## Key Features

1. **Dashboard overview** - Quick metric snapshots from your main dashboard
2. **Human agent** - Team performance when managing customer interactions
3. **AI assistant** - Effectiveness metrics for automated customer support
4. **Sales** - Revenue and conversion data from customer engagement
5. **FAQs** - Self-service content engagement tracking

## Using the Dashboard Overview

Navigate to **Dashboard > Overview** to access basic analytics:

- **Total conversations**: Count of all conversations within your selected timeframe
- **Chat-to-sales rate**: Percentage of interactions resulting in customer purchases

You can filter by time period and click Reload to update metrics.

## Accessing Detailed Analytics

1. Open the **Analytics** section
2. Apply time filters to select your desired date range
3. Use comparison tools to assess performance against previous periods
4. Review trend charts for pattern identification
5. Select Reload to refresh current data
