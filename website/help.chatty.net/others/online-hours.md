# Online Hours

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/others/online-hours

## Availability

This feature is available for all users.

## Overview

This feature enables users to establish online and offline availability windows within their chat system. It allows teams to communicate their support hours to customers, including working schedules, breaks, and time off periods.

## Key Requirement

The online/offline status functionality requires activation of the **Live chat** block within Chatbox style settings to operate properly.

## Setup Process

### Step 1: Define Working Hours
- Option for continuous availability (24/7)
- Option to set custom hours aligned with your schedule

### Step 2: Configure Time Off
- Brief breaks during operating hours
- Holiday periods for full days off

### Step 3: Timezone Selection
Select your timezone to ensure customers see accurate local times. For distributed teams, select the primary time zone where most support is handled.

### Step 4: Status Display Settings
Choose when your online status appears:
- Only during scheduled business hours
- During business hours OR whenever any team member is available

### Step 5: Customize Messages
Create messages customers see when you're available and unavailable. Default options provided:
- "We are online"
- "Usually reply in a few minutes"

### Step 6: Save Changes

## Preview Feature

Users can preview how online/offline status appears in the chatbox before finalizing settings.
