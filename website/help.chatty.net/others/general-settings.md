# General Settings

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/others/general-settings

## Store Name and Avatar Configuration

1. Navigate to **Settings**
2. Select **Manage** in the General section
3. Update your store name and avatar image
4. Click **Save** to apply changes

**Avatar Requirements:** You can upload your own image as avatar. Make sure your image is in the right format (.gif, .jpg, .png) and size less than 200 KB.

## Disclaimer Consent Setup

This feature displays privacy information in your email collection form during conversations, ensuring customers understand data usage before engaging.

1. Access **Settings**
2. Click **Manage** under General
3. Input your disclaimer consent message and privacy policy URL

This transparency measure appears before conversations begin or during subscription processes.

## Follow-Up Email Feature

Enable customers to transition from live chat to email correspondence. Once activated, users see a **Continue on email** option in the chatbox, allowing conversations to persist through email channels.

**Activation:** Toggle the feature on to make this option available to your customers.
