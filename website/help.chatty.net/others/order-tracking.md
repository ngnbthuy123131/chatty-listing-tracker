# Order Tracking

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/others/order-tracking

## Availability

This feature is available for all users.

## Overview

Order tracking enables customers to independently monitor their purchase status and shipment progress through the chatbox, eliminating the need for direct customer service contact.

## Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Enable in Chatbox
Navigate to your Chatbox settings, access the General tab, and activate the Order tracking toggle within the Blocks section.

### Step 2: Select Tracking Method
- Access Settings → Integrations
- Locate Order tracking and select Manage
- Choose your preferred tracking approach

### Step 3: Save Changes
Confirm and save all configuration settings.

## Tracking Method Comparison

| Method | Ideal Use Case | Functionality |
|--------|----------------|---------------|
| Default tracking | Major carriers (DHL, FedEx, UPS) | Directs to carrier's official tracking page |
| Custom tracking | Local/private shipping services | Links to carrier's custom tracking URL |
| 17TRACK | On-site tracking preference | Embeds tracking without leaving your store |

## Customer Usage

**Via Chatbox:**
1. Open the chatbox and select Order tracking
2. Enter either order number (with email/phone) or tracking number
3. Click Track to retrieve status

**During AI Conversation:**
Customers can request tracking information within their chat conversation with the AI assistant.

## Information Displayed

**Order Details:**
- Order number and confirmation date
- Status (digital: Confirmed/Completed; physical: Confirmed/On its way/Delivered)
- Item count and total amount

**Tracking Details:**
- Product images and quantities
- Shipment status progression
- Carrier information and tracking numbers
- Fulfillment date

**Fulfillment Definition:** To fulfill an order means to complete the process of preparing and delivering the items that a customer has purchased.

**Troubleshooting:** Shopify-supported carriers provide real-time updates, while unsupported carriers display basic status information only.
