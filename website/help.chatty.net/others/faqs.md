# Frequently Asked Questions

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/faqs

## 1. What do I need to do after uninstalling Chatty?

When removing the app from your Shopify store, residual code may remain in a disabled state due to immediate access loss to theme files.

### Manual Removal Steps

**Delete the FAQ Page:**
1. Access Shopify Admin → Online store → Pages
2. Locate and select the "Frequently Asked Question" page
3. Click **Actions** → **Delete pages**

## 2. Does Chatty translate questions and answers?

Yes. The platform supports translation of questions and answers. See the [Translation documentation](translation.md) for details.

## 3. I publish a language but I cannot see it on my store

**Resolution:** Ensure your website supports the target language before publishing.

**Configuration steps:**
1. Navigate to Shopify Admin **Settings** → **Language**
2. Configure your desired language

## 4. I set up online hours but cannot see my online status in the chatbox

**Requirement:** The online/offline status functionality only displays when the **Contact us** block is enabled.

**Activation steps:**
1. Go to Widget → General
2. In the Blocks section, enable **Contact us**

## 5. Can I export all questions to my device?

Yes. Questions can be exported as a CSV file through the FAQs section.

**Export process:**
1. Navigate to **FAQs**
2. Select desired questions
3. Click **Export**

## 6. Why can't I connect my Outlook email to Chatty?

**Common cause:** Organizational security policies typically restrict automatic email forwarding by default.

**Solution:** Contact your organization's IT administrator to request forwarding permissions.
