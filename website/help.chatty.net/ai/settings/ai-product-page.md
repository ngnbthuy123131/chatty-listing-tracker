# AI Product Page

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/ai/settings/ai-product-page

## Availability

This feature is available for Pro & Plus plan users.

## Overview

The AI product page is an embedded assistant on product pages featuring an inline chatbox and automatically generated questions. It lets shoppers ask product-specific questions without navigating away from the listing.

## Key Components

- Interactive chat input field
- AI-generated questions tailored to each product
- Context-aware responses

## Benefits

- Displaying AI-suggested inquiries relevant to the specific item
- Providing quick answers about product details
- Maintaining focus without disruptive pop-ups

## How It Functions

1. AI examines product data (description, variants, specifications, pricing)
2. System creates 3-5 customized questions for that product
3. Questions appear alongside a text input field
4. Shoppers select suggested questions or type their own
5. AI provides instant, product-specific answers

## Setup Requirements

- AI assistant activation
- Product data synced to training data
- Additional store and product information added

## Configuration Steps

1. Navigate to AI Assistant section
2. Access Settings
3. Enable "Embed product AI assistant" and save
4. Select "Go to theme"
5. Confirm AI assistant block appears on product pages
6. Adjust block styling as needed
7. Save changes

## Important Notes

Product information updates won't automatically refresh generated questions. Contact support for the latest versions.

## FAQs

**Does this require active AI assistant?** Yes, the embedded chat depends on your activated AI assistant and uses identical data sources.

**Can suggested questions be manually edited?** Questions generate automatically per product. Enhance them by improving product descriptions with detailed feature information.

**Does this replace the main chatbox?** No, it complements your primary chat widget. Customers access the full chatbox separately.
