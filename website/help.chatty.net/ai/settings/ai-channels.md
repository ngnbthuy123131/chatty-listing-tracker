# AI Channels

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/ai/settings/ai-channels

## Overview

AI channels allow your AI assistant to automatically respond to customer messages across multiple communication platforms, including email, Facebook Messenger, Instagram, and WhatsApp.

## Key Benefits

- Deliver round-the-clock customer assistance across all platforms
- Cut down message response times on social channels and email
- Keep a uniform tone throughout communications
- Seize additional revenue from social conversations
- Allow staff to concentrate on intricate support issues

## Prerequisites

1. Set up and configured your AI assistant
2. Linked the communication channels where automation should function

## Activation Steps

Access your AI settings and navigate to the channels configuration area. Toggle automatic replies for:
- **Email**: Activates automatic message responses via email
- **Messenger**: Enables replies on Facebook Messenger across connected accounts
- **Instagram**: Turns on direct message automation
- **WhatsApp**: Prepares WhatsApp response automation

Save your configuration to complete setup.

## Important Details

- Response timing occurs within a 5-25 second window, permitting staff intervention
- All AI messages consume your subscription's reply allocation
- Social platforms display product links rather than formatted cards
- Team members may assume control of conversations whenever needed
