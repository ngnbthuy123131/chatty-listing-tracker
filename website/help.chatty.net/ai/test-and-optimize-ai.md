# Test & Optimize AI

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/ai/test-and-optimize-ai

## Overview

This feature is available to all users. The AI assistant is currently in beta; users can contact support with feedback.

## Testing Your AI Assistant

### Purpose

AI test zone helps simulate customer interactions before going live, ensuring your AI responds appropriately to customer inquiries.

### Prerequisites

Before testing, complete these setup steps:
- Add data sources (FAQs, product information, etc.)
- Configure custom instructions (tone, voice, response length)
- Establish other settings (chatbot name, avatar, etc.)

**Note:** You can test the AI without enabling it live if you've added data sources.

### Testing Steps

**Step 1: Access Test Zone**
Navigate to AI assistant → Test

**Step 2: Chat with Your AI**
Interact with the assistant as a customer would. Use provided question examples or request new ones via "Regenerate questions."

**Testing tips:**
- Evaluate one topic at a time
- Phrase questions multiple ways
- Verify response length appropriateness

**Step 3: Review & Improve Responses**

Evaluate these aspects:
- Information accuracy
- Professional yet friendly tone
- Accurate product details
- Correct pricing and availability

Review which data sources the AI consulted for each response.

## Optimizing AI Responses

### Section A: Unresolved Questions

**Definition:** Customer inquiries the AI couldn't answer effectively. These appear when users select "Talk to a person."

**Timeframe:** System displays unresolved questions from the past 30 days only.

#### Optimization Process

**Step 1:** Navigate to Unresolved Questions

**Step 2: Analyze Patterns**
- Identify training data gaps
- Group similar questions to create comprehensive answers
- Discover new FAQs for your help center

**Step 3: Add Answers**
Select a question and click "Add answer" → enter your response. Answers are added to your data sources in the Question list.

**Step 4: Retest**
Return to Test zone and verify the AI now handles the question properly.

**Spam handling:** Click "Ignore" to remove duplicate or spam questions.

### Section B: AI Response Review

Each response includes a "Review sources" button showing which information was used.

**Use this to:**
- Supplement missing product information
- Fix inaccurate policy details
- Enhance incomplete answers
- Include question variations the AI missed

#### Action Steps

**Identify Inaccuracies:** When you spot errors or gaps, click "Add relevant data" to access the data sources page and insert corrections.

**Monitor Patterns:** Track repeated correction types. Create comprehensive topic entries rather than fragmentary additions to boost overall performance.
