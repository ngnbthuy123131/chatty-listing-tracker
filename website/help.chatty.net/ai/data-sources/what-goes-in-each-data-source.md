# What Goes in Each Data Source

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/ai/data-sources/what-goes-in-each-data-source

## 1. Q&A

**Best for:** Addressing frequently asked customer questions

**What to include:**
- Policies (returns, shipping, warranties)
- Business details (hours, location, contact info)
- Troubleshooting guides

**What to exclude:**
- Product specs like pricing or descriptions (automatic)
- Lengthy documents (use Files)
- Behavioral guidelines (use Custom Instructions)

*Example:* "Return items within 30 days, unworn with tags. Free return shipping for exchanges. Start returns through your order email."

**Note:** Product-specific advice should go in product descriptions or metafields, not Q&A.

## 2. General Instructions

**Best for:** Defining AI personality and response patterns

**What to include:**
- Brand voice and tone guidance
- Response formatting preferences
- Industry-specific expertise positioning
- Decision-making frameworks
- Sales strategies and complementary suggestions
- Behavioral boundaries
- Output format specifications

**What to exclude:**
- Specific facts (use Q&A)
- Basic product information (automatic)
- Lengthy policy text (use Q&A)
- Scenario-based instructions (use Scenarios)

*Example:* "Be helpful and friendly. When sizing questions arise, reference guides and assist if customers are between sizes."

## 3. Scenarios

**Best for:** Handling specific situations and customer journeys

**What to include:**
- Customer segments (first-time, returning, bulk buyers)
- Shopping contexts (gifts, urgent orders, special events)
- Issue resolution approaches (damaged shipments, sizing errors)
- Sales circumstances (cart abandonment, pricing)
- Time-based flows (holiday deadlines, seasonal shopping)

**What to exclude:**
- Simple Q&A exchanges (use Questions)
- General behavior rules (use Instructions)

*Example:* Multi-step dissatisfaction response including acknowledgment, apology, ownership, solutions, and escalation.

## 4. URLs

**Best for:** Directing to existing external resources

**What to include:**
- Size guides and fit charts
- Care instruction pages
- Video tutorials
- Comprehensive FAQ pages
- Policy documentation pages

**What to exclude:**
- Links prone to frequent changes
- Password-protected resources
- Interactive or embedded content

## 5. Files

**Best for:** Comprehensive documents and bulk information

**What to include:**
- Detailed care and maintenance guides
- Product manuals
- Technical specifications
- Compatibility matrices
- Training materials
- Compiled FAQs

**What to exclude:**
- Individual Q&A pairs (use Questions)
- Basic policies (use Q&A)
- Files exceeding 2MB or with complex formatting

## 6. Smart Recommendations

**Best for:** Guiding AI product suggestions

**What to include:**
- Product lists for specific scenarios (bestsellers, new arrivals, sales, occasions)

## Key Reminders

- **Common Error:** Consolidating all content into one category rather than distributing appropriately.
- Eliminate redundant information across categories
- Test AI responses after adding content
