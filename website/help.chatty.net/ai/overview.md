# AI Assistant Overview

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/ai/overview

## Availability

This feature is accessible to all users.

## What is AI Assistant?

The AI assistant expedites customer support by providing 24/7 responses trained on your specific business data. Key benefits include:

- Faster resolution times
- Happier customers
- Less strain on your support team

The system maintains your brand's distinctive voice across all interactions, even for solo entrepreneurs.

**Prerequisite:** Enable live chat functionality via Chatbox settings to activate the AI assistant.

## Implementation Steps

### Step 1: Train Your AI Assistant
The platform auto-syncs existing store data for training. Additional data sources can be manually incorporated through the data sources and training guides.

### Step 2: Customize Your AI Assistant
Configure settings including:
- Bot appearance (name, avatar)
- Communication guidelines (tone, response length, voice)
- Availability windows based on team schedules
- Human handoff preferences

### Step 3: Test & Optimize
Evaluate AI responses and refine performance through testing protocols.

### Step 4: Activate
Enable the AI assistant to begin automating support operations.

### Step 5: Continuous Improvement
Ongoing testing and optimization occurs during active use.

## Operational Features

When active:
- All conversations will be answered automatically by AI assistant
- Team members can join conversations anytime
- Upon human transfer, the AI generates "Chat summary" highlighting language, key issues, and suggested responses
