# How Chatty Trains AI

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/ai/overview/how-chatty-trains-ai

## Understanding AI Training

Chatty's AI training involves three core concepts:

- **AI training**: Teaching the assistant to comprehend your business context and answer customer inquiries appropriately
- **Data sources**: The information types you supply to help train the AI
- **Learning process**: How the AI processes and learns from your data to generate relevant responses

## The Three-Stage Training Process

### Stage 1: Data Processing

When you add data sources, the system:

1. Analyzes your data to identify key information
2. Breaks down complex information into smaller, manageable pieces
3. Organizes the information into a structured format the AI can understand

### Stage 2: Context Building

The AI assistant then:

1. Creates connections between related pieces of information
2. Develops understanding of your specific business terminology
3. Learns your brand voice and communication style

### Stage 3: Response Generation

Finally, the AI assistant:

1. Understands customer question and extracts the right information or keyword
2. Uses the processed information to create accurate responses
3. Maintains consistency with your brand voice
4. Provides relevant product recommendations and solutions

## Supported Data Sources

- FAQs and help articles
- Product information (description, variants, price, etc.)
- Other custom training responses
- Customer service conversation history (coming soon)
- Website content (coming soon)

## Best Practices for AI Training

### 1. Prioritize Important Information

Begin by adding your most critical content:
- FAQs and essential policies
- Top product information
- Custom instructions for specific AI guidance

### 2. Maintain Current Information

- Regularly review and update data sources
- Remove outdated information

**Note:** Product information updates daily at 12:00 AM PST.

### 3. Monitor and Refine Results

- Test and review AI responses regularly
- Add new training data based on customer interactions

## Privacy and Security Standards

- All data is encrypted during processing
- Customer information is handled according to privacy regulations
- You maintain control over what data is used for training
