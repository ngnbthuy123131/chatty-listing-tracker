# What AI Assistant Can Do

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/ai/overview/what-ai-assistant-can-do

## Availability

This feature is accessible to all users.

## Overview

The AI assistant accelerates customer support by leveraging provided data to address inquiries efficiently.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Instant Response
Responds to customer questions instantly 24/7.

### 2. Product Recommendation
The system facilitates product discovery through:
- Conversational needs assessment
- Item suggestions matched to customer requirements
- Comparative product analysis
- Detailed specifications and information
- Alternative options when primary choices are unavailable

### 3. Order Support (Coming Soon)
Planned features include:
- Order tracking assistance
- Status updates and notifications
- Shipping timeline and delivery information
- Order history location

### 4. Multilingual Support
- Responds in multiple languages
- Interprets rephrased inquiries
- Maintains consistent messaging across languages

**Limitation:** AI may have difficulty with special characters, symbols, or highly technical industry-specific terms.

### 5. Conversation Summarization
When escalating to human agents, the system generates:
- Complete conversation summaries
- Automatic language detection
- Key issues and customer needs highlighting
- Suggested response guidance

### 6. Shopping Cart Updates
AI modifies carts by adding/removing items directly, with potential compatibility notes for custom-built themes.

### 7. Checkout Link Delivery
The system can transmit checkout links reflecting current cart contents.

## Custom Instruction Capabilities

Training options enable:
- Brand-aligned communication tone and style
- Response length customization
- Feature emphasis selection
- Inquiry-specific protocols
- Personalized service policies
