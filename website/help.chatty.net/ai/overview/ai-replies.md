# AI Replies

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/ai/overview/ai-replies

## Availability

This feature is accessible to all Chatty users.

## What Are AI Replies?

AI replies are responses generated by Chatty AI assistant when it answers customer questions automatically. Each message sent by the AI counts toward your monthly quota.

## Why This Matters

- **Cost management**: Select appropriate plans and prevent surprise fees
- **Usage planning**: Identify patterns to anticipate peak periods
- **Performance insights**: Evaluate AI's role in customer interactions
- **Budget control**: Set spending limits to keep AI costs predictable and manageable

## Calculation Method

**What counts:**
- Direct responses to customer inquiries
- Product recommendations
- Follow-up explanations

**What doesn't count:**
- Initial AI greeting messages
- System notifications
- Automation-triggered messages
- Widget FAQ suggestions
- Unanswered questions lacking knowledge

## Pricing Structure

| Plan | Monthly Quota | Overage Rate |
|------|---------------|--------------|
| Free | 100 | N/A |
| Basic | 1,000 | $0.04 |
| Pro | 5,000 | $0.038 |
| Plus | 10,000 | $0.025 |

## Monitoring Usage

Access the Subscription dashboard to review total usage status. Usage resets on your billing date, not calendar month.

## Setting Spending Limits

Navigate to Subscription → Set usage limit → Enable additional charges → enter budget amount. Set your limit 15-20% above expected usage to handle busy periods.

## When Limits Are Reached

AI stops responding to new conversations until the next billing cycle begins, though human agents continue serving customers.
