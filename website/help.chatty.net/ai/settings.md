# AI Settings

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/ai/settings

## Appearance

Users can customize their AI assistant by providing a specific name and avatar, which will display in the chatbox interface.

## Welcome Message

The welcome message serves as the initial greeting customers receive when they click "Contact us."

## Custom Instruction

This feature enables specific guidance on how the AI assistant should interact with customers, allowing control over inquiry handling.

### Personalization Options

Custom instructions help align the assistant with:

- Specific business needs
- Unique brand voice
- Common inquiries for your store
- Special handling for certain question types
- Particular scenario responses

### Implementation Methods

**Manual Addition:** Navigate to AI assistant → Instruction tab → Enter custom instruction.

**AI-Generated Instructions:** You can generate custom instructions for your store with AI.

### Organization Tips

Multiple instructions can be included together, organized clearly by topic or scenario to aid AI comprehension.
