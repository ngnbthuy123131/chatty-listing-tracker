# Embedded Chatbox

> Source: https://help.chatty.net/chatbox/embedded-chatbox

## Availability

This feature is accessible to all users.

## Overview

The embedded chatbox feature allows merchants to integrate a complete chat interface directly into store pages as a full-width section. Unlike the floating chatbox button, this approach weaves the entire chat experience seamlessly into your page layout.

### Ideal Use Cases

- Dedicated support or contact pages
- Product information pages
- FAQ pages with live support access

## Comparison: Floating vs. Embedded

| Aspect | Floating Chatbox | Embedded Chatbox |
|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Display** | Button that opens overlay | Full interface built into page |
| **Visibility** | Available on all pages | Only on pages where you add it |
| **Space Usage** | Floats over content | Takes up section space on page |
| **Best For** | Store-wide availability | Dedicated support pages |

You can deploy both versions simultaneously. For instance, maintain floating chat across your entire store while adding an embedded version specifically to your contact page.

## Implementation Steps

1. Navigate to Chatbox settings
2. Locate the **Embedded chatbox** option within the **Advanced** tab
3. Select **Go to theme**
4. You'll access your theme editor; decide the placement for your chatbox
5. Click **Add section**
6. Click **Save** to finalize
