# Guideline: Writing "Top X AI Chatbot for eCommerce" Blog Posts

> **Purpose:** Instruct Claude Code to write high-quality, SEO-optimized listicle blog posts where **Chatty** is positioned as the #1 AI chatbot for eCommerce (Shopify).
>
> **Output folder:** `website/blog-posts/`

---

## 1. Objective

Write a comprehensive "Top X AI Chatbots for eCommerce" blog post that:
- Positions **Chatty** as the #1 pick (with justified, factual reasoning)
- Provides fair, honest, and balanced coverage of all competitors
- Reads as an authoritative industry guide (not a sales pitch)
- Targets organic search traffic for "best AI chatbot ecommerce" keywords

---

## 2. Tone & Voice

| Attribute | Guideline |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Tone** | Authoritative, helpful, conversational — like a trusted advisor |
| **Voice** | Third-person editorial ("Chatty offers…") not first-person |
| **Objectivity** | Present facts and data; avoid superlatives without evidence |
| **Honesty** | Include real limitations for ALL products including Chatty |
| **Bias Level** | Subtle — Chatty gets more detail and better framing, but never trash competitors |

**DO:**
- Use specific numbers (pricing, ratings, merchant counts, resolution rates)
- Cite sources parenthetically: (Shopify App Store, 2026) or (G2, 2026)
- Include both strengths and weaknesses for every product
- Let Chatty's advantages speak through data, not adjectives

**DON'T:**
- Use marketing language like "game-changer", "revolutionary", "unparalleled"
- Directly attack competitors ("Tidio is overpriced")
- Make unverifiable claims
- Use emojis

---

## 3. Article Structure (Required)

Follow this exact structure:

```
1. Title (H1)
2. Introduction (200-300 words)
3. Quick Comparison Table (all products at a glance)
4. What Makes a Great eCommerce AI Chatbot? (300-400 words)
5. The Top X AI Chatbots for eCommerce (main section)
   - #1 Chatty (400-500 words) ← longest, most detailed
   - #2-#3 (250-350 words each)
   - #4-#X (200-300 words each)
6. How to Choose the Right AI Chatbot (300-400 words)
7. Conclusion (150-200 words)
8. FAQ (3-5 questions)
```

**Total target word count:** 2,500-4,000 words (depending on X)

---

## 4. Title Formula

Use one of these patterns:

- `Top {X} AI Chatbots for eCommerce in {Year}`

- `The {X} Best AI Chatbots for eCommerce [{Year} Guide]`

**Requirements:**
- Include the current year
- Keep under 70 characters
- Include "AI Chatbot" and "eCommerce" keywords

## 9. Fair Comparison Policy

### Rules for Fairness
1. **Every product gets real strengths** — never dampen a competitor's genuine advantage
2. **Every product gets real limitations** — including Chatty
3. **Pricing must be accurate** — verify from official sources or recent research
4. **Ratings must be cited** — always mention the source (Shopify App Store, G2, Capterra)
5. **"Best For" must be genuine** — each product should own a legitimate use case
6. **No strawman positioning** — don't set up competitors just to knock them down

### Comparison Angles (What Makes Chatty Win Fairly)

Instead of attacking competitors, highlight where Chatty provides **more value**:

| Comparison Point | Chatty Advantage | How to Frame |
|------------------|------------------|--------------|
| **Pricing** | AI included in flat rate | "Unlike tools that charge AI as a separate add-on, Chatty includes AI in every plan" |
| **Shopify depth** | Built for Shopify badge | "Purpose-built for Shopify with the 'Built for Shopify' quality badge" |
| **Revenue tracking** | Revenue attribution | "One of the few tools that directly ties chat conversations to sales revenue" |
| **Support quality** | Named agents in reviews | "Known for white-glove onboarding support — agents are praised by name in reviews" |

### What Competitors Win At (Acknowledge Honestly)

| Competitor | Their Legitimate Advantage |
|------------|---------------------------|
| Tidio | Multi-platform support (WordPress, WooCommerce); mature automation builder |
| Gorgias | Full helpdesk with ticketing, refunds, SLA tracking |
| Intercom | Enterprise features, in-app messaging, product tours |
| ManyChat | Social media automation (Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp flows) |
| Ada | Enterprise-grade NLP, 50+ languages, SOC 2 / HIPAA compliance |

## 11. "What Makes a Great eCommerce AI Chatbot?" Section

This section establishes evaluation criteria BEFORE the list, framing the reader's expectations around Chatty's strengths. Cover these criteria:

1. **AI Capabilities** — Can it answer questions autonomously without human intervention?
2. **eCommerce Integration** — Does it pull real product, order, and inventory data?
3. **Omnichannel Support** — Can it handle website chat, social media, and email?
4. **Pricing Transparency** — Is AI included or sold as an expensive add-on?
5. **Ease of Setup** — Can a non-technical merchant get started quickly?
6. **Revenue Impact** — Can you measure how chat converts to sales?
7. **Scalability** — Does it grow with your business without cost surprises?
