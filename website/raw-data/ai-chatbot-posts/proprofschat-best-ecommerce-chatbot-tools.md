## FAQs

**What is an eCommerce chatbot?**
An eCommerce chatbot is a computer program designed to simulate human conversation on eCommerce websites or apps. It assists customers by providing product information, answering queries, facilitating purchases, and offering a personalized shopping experience.

**What are the benefits of eCommerce chatbots?**
eCommerce chatbots offer 24/7 customer support, improve user engagement, enhance shopping experiences, and boost sales. They provide instant responses, recommend products, and streamline the buying process.

**How to choose an eCommerce chatbot?**
Consider your budget, integration capabilities, customization options, and scalability. Evaluate the chatbot's natural language processing abilities, user experience, and analytics features to ensure it aligns with your specific business needs and goals.
