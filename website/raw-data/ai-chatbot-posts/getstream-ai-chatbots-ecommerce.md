# AI Chatbots for eCommerce: Best Platforms, Examples & Use Cases

> Source: https://getstream.io/blog/ai-chatbots-ecommerce/

## Overview

Throughout the eCommerce customer journey, from product discovery to post-purchase support, numerous revenue opportunities exist. Selecting the right AI chatbot provider requires thorough analysis balancing functionality, integration capabilities, and scalability.

The best platforms enable custom workflows, deliver rapid personalized responses, and eliminate friction points in the buying experience.

## What Are AI Chatbots for eCommerce?

AI chatbots for eCommerce function as virtual assistants enhancing customer experiences in online shopping environments. Unlike traditional rule-based systems, they leverage natural language processing and large language models to interpret buyer intent, enabling them to:

- Answer customer questions
- Guide purchasing decisions
- Provide support services

This technology drives conversational commerce, where customers engage with AI assistants across websites and social media channels. With widespread AI adoption and cart abandonment rates averaging over 70%, these tools have become essential for revenue generation.

## Platform Detailed Profiles

### Ada

Ada employs LLMs to automate customer interactions with contextual, scalable support throughout the journey. The platform emphasizes GDPR, SOC 2, and HIPAA compliance with privacy-first design.

**Key Features:**
- Natural Language Understanding for contextualized responses
- Support for 50+ languages
- Sandbox testing for conversational capabilities before launch

**Ideal For:** Multilingual support; maintaining service during traffic spikes

**Differentiator:** Low-code interface allows product managers to build and adjust flows without technical resources.

### Zowie

This platform automates workflows for support teams, focusing on reducing repetitive query volumes. It delivers pre-built flows and real-time escalation.

**Key Features:**
- Pre-built order status and returns flows
- Proprietary accuracy control layers
- Real-time escalation capabilities
- Built-in analytics dashboard

**Ideal For:** High support volumes; order system and inventory tracking

**Differentiator:** Primarily serves EMEA eCommerce businesses with 100+ language support.

### Gorgias

Gorgias powers automated chat and ranks as the top-rated Shopify helpdesk. It integrates with storefront platforms to incorporate live order data into conversations.

**Key Features:**
- Agents for FAQs, order tracking, and returns
- Unified inbox across all channels
- Intent detection capabilities

**Ideal For:** DTC brands using Shopify

**Differentiator:** Consolidates live chat, email, SMS, and social messages into one interface, enabling handling of refunds and discounts without system switching.

### Certainly

Combining no-code setup tools with developer API integrations, Certainly accelerates AI chatbot deployment while enabling customization.

**Key Features:**
- Personalized product suggestions linked to catalog knowledge
- In-chat checkout functionality
- Proactive and automated cart recovery prompts

**Ideal For:** Reducing cart abandonment rates

**Differentiator:** Purpose-built for online retailers, focusing specifically on conversion rate improvement throughout the customer journey.

### Tidio

An all-in-one, no-code AI chatbot platform designed for small to medium eCommerce companies implementing live chat.

**Key Features:**
- Chatbot training using existing FAQs
- Visual flow builder interface
- Multi-channel conversation inbox
- Behavior-based proactive messaging

**Ideal For:** eCommerce teams with minimal engineering support

**Differentiator:** Requires no engineering intervention, prioritizing speed to market and functionality over customization.

### Netomi

This AI-powered customer experience platform provides a no-code studio for rapid AI chatbot deployment, supporting both chat and email inquiries.

**Key Features:**
- LLM-powered responses trained on customer data and brand guidelines
- Co-pilot mode for agent message review and editing
- Autopilot mode for complete automation

**Ideal For:** Scalable AI-powered support; regulated industries requiring governance

**Differentiator:** Enables strict controls on AI chatbots based on brand guidelines and regulatory requirements.

### LivePerson

A conversational AI platform combining voice and text automation with real-time human agent routing for customer conversations.

**Key Features:**
- No-code conversational builder with structured workflows
- Built-in intent detection and personalization
- Omnichannel messaging across web, SMS, WhatsApp
- Real-time automation and escalation analytics

**Ideal For:** Complex operations requiring routine automation with exception escalation

**Differentiator:** Provides enterprise-grade controls with serverless logic layers for detailed conversation routing rules.

### Stream

Stream's developer-friendly APIs embed real-time voice, chat, and video features without infrastructure complexity. AI chatbots and voice agents integrate through Stream's chat and video APIs.

**Key Features:**
- Multi-framework support (React, Flutter, Swift, Kotlin)
- Pre-built and customizable UI components
- Scalable global edge network minimizing latency

**Ideal For:** Voice and text use cases; in-app support; scalable mobile and web apps

**Differentiator:** Full control over conversational experience design without custom infrastructure building.

### ManyChat

ManyChat automates interactions on social media and messaging channels, supporting eCommerce growth through campaign-style chat flows.

**Key Features:**
- Drag-and-drop flow builder
- Natural language parsing for lightweight FAQs
- Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp, and SMS integration
- Marketing campaign deployment and drip sequences

**Ideal For:** Small and medium eCommerce leveraging social commerce and influencer sales

**Differentiator:** Prioritizes sales and marketing efforts over customer support.

## Real-World Implementation Examples

### Alibaba's AliMe Chatbot
Alibaba's AI chatbot manages 75% of customer inquiries across nearly 1 billion customers, boosting satisfaction by 25%. Deep order and logistics system integration enables independent handling of delivery updates and returns processing.

### Vodafone's SuperTOBi Assistant
SuperTOBi handles over half of first-contact customer service queries, managing everything from billing issues to technical support. The system streamlines online plan changes, boosting revenue while reducing agent workload.

### Amarra Fashion Wholesale
Using ChatGPT, Amarra generated product descriptions and managed inventory content, reducing overstock by 40%. The chatbot now handles approximately three-quarters of customer queries, improving operational efficiency and satisfaction.

## Primary Use Cases

### Product Recommendations
Bots analyze browsing habits, sentiment, and inferred intent to guide discovery and personalize recommendations. Advanced NLP enables back-and-forth conversation for context clarification, reducing friction between discovery and purchase while increasing order values.

### Customer Support
AI-powered support interprets intent dynamically for personalized, empathetic responses. Systems detect when complex queries require human escalation, maintaining frictionless customer experiences.

### Engagement & Retention
Automation reduces cart abandonment and sends timely post-purchase communications and campaigns. Conversation-based loyalty efforts—triggered by behavior patterns—significantly boost conversion and lifetime value metrics.

### Promotional Messaging
Context-aware messaging drives upsells and cross-sells at optimal customer journey moments, surpassing batch email campaign effectiveness.
