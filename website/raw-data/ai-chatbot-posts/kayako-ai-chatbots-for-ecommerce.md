# Top 6 AI Chatbots for Ecommerce: Evaluated by Industry Experts

> Source: https://kayako.com/blog/ai-chatbots-for-ecommerce/

## Overview

This comprehensive review evaluates six AI chatbot platforms specifically for ecommerce applications. The author conducted 90-day real-world testing across actual store environments to assess performance beyond marketing claims.

## Research Methodology

The evaluation framework examined:
- Core features and AI sophistication
- Integration capabilities with ecommerce platforms
- Usability and setup ease
- Customer support responsiveness
- Cost-effectiveness and scalability
- User feedback from G2, Capterra, Trustpilot
- Analytics and reporting depth

---

## 1. Kayako – Best AI Chatbot for E-commerce

**Description:** An AI-first customer support platform designed to eliminate repetitive tickets through autonomous resolution.

### Key Features
- Autonomous resolution engine for WISMO (Where Is My Order) queries
- Real-time integration with order management and logistics systems
- Multilingual support across 100+ languages
- Multichannel availability (web, mobile, email)
- Self-learning capabilities from closed tickets
- Pay-per-resolution pricing model ($1/ticket)

### Kayako Pros
- "Handles 8,000+ tickets autonomously from 10,000 monthly volume"
- Native retail integrations fetch live order and inventory data
- Eliminates context loss during handoffs
- Unique cost model tied to resolution, not seats

### Kayako Cons
- Best suited for brands handling 5,000+ tickets/month
- Limited marketing automation features
- Web-first platform (social messaging under development)

### Pricing Model
Only charges when AI fully resolves a ticket; no seat-based fees.

- Detailed performance analytics

### Gorgias Pros
- Efficient ticket management and routing

## Selection Criteria Guide

When choosing an ecommerce AI chatbot, consider:

1. **Business Scale:** Startups favor Tidio/ManyChat; enterprises benefit from Ada/Kayako
2. **Integration Needs:** Verify compatibility with your ecommerce platform and CRM tools
3. **Support Complexity:** Simple FAQs work with basic bots; complex queries require advanced NLP
4. **Primary Goal:** Sales-focused teams choose Intercom/Drift; support optimization prefers Gorgias/Kayako
5. **Multichannel Requirements:** ManyChat excels across messaging platforms
6. **AI Sophistication:** Evaluate customization depth and language understanding
7. **Budget vs. ROI:** Balance upfront costs against automation benefits
8. **Analytics Capabilities:** Prioritize platforms with robust performance tracking

## Conclusion

Selecting the right chatbot is a critical investment with the power to transform your ecommerce customer journey. The choice should align with immediate operational needs and broader strategic ecommerce engagement goals.
