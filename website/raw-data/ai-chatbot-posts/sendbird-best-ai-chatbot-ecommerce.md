# The 10 Best AI Chatbots for Ecommerce in 2025

> Source: https://sendbird.com/blog/best-ai-chatbot-ecommerce

## Overview

This comprehensive guide from Sendbird examines leading AI chatbot solutions for online retailers, covering use cases, essential features, and detailed product comparisons.

## What is an AI Chatbot for Ecommerce?

An ecommerce AI chatbot is software that enhances online shopping through natural language processing. Unlike rule-based predecessors, these tools deliver "human-like conversations" and manage sophisticated workflows across the customer journey.

## 6 Key Use Cases

### 1. Personalized Recommendations
AI chatbots function as shopping assistants, suggesting products based on customer behavior and preferences. Research indicates "80% of consumers are more likely to purchase from brands that offer a personalized experience."

### 2. Lead Generation
When integrated with CRM systems, chatbots collect visitor information and encourage signups. Studies show "55% of companies using chatbots report increased high-quality leads."

### 3. Cart Abandonment Prevention
Proactive engagement reduces lost sales. Sephora achieved "11% increase in conversions" using its Facebook Messenger chatbot.

### 4. 24/7 Customer Support
Immediate assistance across multiple channels reduces support costs. Data shows "76% of shoppers prefer self-serve resources before contacting representatives."

### 5. Customer Feedback Collection
Chatbots gather shopping experience data through surveys and ratings without manual effort.

### 6. Enhanced Experience
Personalization and accessibility create differentiation and strengthen customer loyalty.

## Essential Features for Ecommerce Chatbots

- **CRM Integration**: Seamless connection with Shopify, Salesforce, and third-party databases
- **Omnichannel Support**: Availability on websites, apps, and platforms like WhatsApp and Instagram
- **Advanced AI**: Powered by models like ChatGPT, Claude, or Llama
- **Analytics**: Detailed performance tracking and interaction insights

## Best Practices

- **Enable Real-Time Data Syncing**: Connect chatbots with CRM systems and product databases to ensure current information
- **Train Your Chatbot**: Upload company-specific information including products, policies, and pricing for accurate responses
- **Customize Appearance**: Match brand aesthetics and voice through no-code builders
- **Pre-Set Critical Responses**: Define answers to essential business questions while leveraging AI flexibility
- **Monitor and Optimize**: Track accuracy, engagement metrics, and performance KPIs to maximize ROI
