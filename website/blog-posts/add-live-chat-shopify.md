## Want to add live chat to Shopify for free? Try Shopify Inbox

If you’re looking to dip your toes into the world of live chat without spending a dime, [Shopify’s own Shopify Inbox app](https://apps.shopify.com/inbox) is the perfect place to start. It’s completely free to use, integrates seamlessly with your store, and gives you a direct line to your customers.

However, since it’s a free tool, it has some limitations you should know about:

-   **No advanced AI:** While you can set up basic instant answers for common questions, it doesn’t have a smart AI that can learn your products, make recommendations, or upsell for you.
-   **Limited to your store**: It only works for chat on your Shopify site. You can’t manage conversations from Facebook Messenger, Instagram, or WhatsApp in one place.
-   **Basic automation**: You won’t find advanced automation like [proactive live chat](https://chatty.net/blog/what-is-proactive-live-chat) that trigger based on visitor behavior (e.g., time on page or cart value).

**Simple analytics**: The reporting features are minimal, so you won’t get deep insights into team performance or resolution times.

![chatbox customer order conversation](https://chatty.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/chatbox-customer-order-conversation.webp)

Image source: Shopify

Here’s how to get it running in just a few minutes:

**Step 1: Turn on the chat feature**

First, you need to enable the chat widget in your store’s theme.

-   From your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store > Themes**.
-   Find your current theme and click **Customize**.
-   On the left sidebar, click on **App embeds** and then select **Shopify Inbox**.
-   Simply toggle the Online store chat button on.

![online store chatbox preview](https://chatty.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/online-store-chatbox-preview.webp)

**Step 2: Customize the chat button’s appearance**

Make the chat button match your brand’s look and feel.

-   In the same **Shopify Inbox section**, look for **Brand customization**.

Here, you can change the **Color**, select a new **Icon** (like a chat bubble or smiley face), and choose a **Label** (like “Chat” or “Need help?”).

![brand customization chatbox options](https://chatty.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/brand-customization-chatbox-options.webp)

**Step 3: Set your greeting message**

Create a warm, welcoming message that customers see when they first open the chat.

-   Scroll down to the **Greeting message** text box.
-   Write a custom message like, “Hello! Let us know if you have any questions.” Keep in mind that a custom message won’t automatically translate for international visitors.

![chatbox greeting message settings](https://chatty.net/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/chatbox-greeting-message-settings.webp)

**Step 4: Save and you’re live!**

Once you’re happy with your settings, just click the **Save** button in the top right corner. That’s it! Your live chat widget is now active on your storefront, ready to help you connect with customers.

## FAQs about adding live chat to Shopify

### Does live chat really help increase conversions on Shopify?

Yes. Customers who engage with live chat are 70% more likely to make a purchase, and they typically spend 10-15% more per order. Live chat addresses hesitations in real-time, builds trust, and removes purchase barriers that cause cart abandonment. Studies show that 40% of customers who use live chat before checkout end up converting.

### What’s the difference between live chat and a chatbot on Shopify?

Live chat connects customers with real human agents who can handle complex questions and provide personalized service. Chatbots are AI-powered tools that provide instant, automated responses to common questions 24/7. While chatbots excel at handling simple FAQs and basic tasks, live chat agents can solve complex problems, show empathy, and build relationships. Many businesses use both together: chatbots for initial filtering and live agents for detailed support.

### Does Shopify come with built-in live chat?

Yes, Shopify offers Shopify Inbox, a free live chat app that integrates seamlessly with your store. However, it has limitations like no advanced AI, basic automation, and simple analytics. It only works on your Shopify site and doesn’t integrate with social media channels like Facebook Messenger or WhatsApp.

### Do I need to be available 24/7 for live chat to be effective?

Not necessarily. While 24/7 availability captures more leads and helps global customers, you can still see great results during your regular business hours. The key is responding quickly when you are online—customers who get responses [within 5-10 seconds have satisfaction rates over 84%.](https://www.livechat.com/success/key-live-chat-statistics/) Many businesses combine limited-hour live agents with chatbots for after-hours support.

### Should I use AI to answer customers in live chat?

AI can be incredibly effective for handling routine questions, product recommendations, and initial customer screening. However, complex issues, emotional situations, and high-value sales conversations still benefit from human agents. The best approach is often hybrid: let AI handle simple queries and route complex issues to human agents. This gives you the efficiency of automation with the personal touch customers value.
