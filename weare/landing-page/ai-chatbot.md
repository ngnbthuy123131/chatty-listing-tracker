## Section 10: FAQ

**Label:** Common Questions

**Section headline:**
### Everything you want to know about Chatty AI

**Q: What AI model does Chatty use, and is it reliable?**
Chatty is powered by **OpenAI's GPT-4** — the same technology behind ChatGPT, used by millions of people and Fortune 500 companies worldwide. GPT-4 is the most capable language model available today, known for understanding complex questions, nuanced context, and producing natural-sounding responses. When combined with your store's specific product data, it creates an AI assistant that's both intelligent and accurate to your catalog.

**Q: Will AI give wrong answers about my products?**
Chatty AI only answers based on the data you provide — your product catalog, FAQs, custom Q&A pairs, uploaded files, and URLs. It doesn't invent information from the internet. You can also set custom AI instructions to control exactly what topics AI discusses and how it responds. And with the automatic human handoff feature, any question AI isn't confident about gets transferred to your team with full context. You're always in control.

**Q: How is Chatty different from Tidio's Lyro or Gorgias AI?**
Three key differences: **1) Sales-first vs. support-first.** Tidio and Gorgias position as support tools that resolve tickets. Chatty positions as a sales assistant that converts conversations into revenue. **2) Flat-rate pricing.** Tidio charges per-seat plus AI add-ons, and Gorgias charges per-ticket. As AI handles more conversations, their costs go up. Chatty's flat rate stays the same. **3) Shopify-native.** Chatty is built exclusively for Shopify with the "Built for Shopify" badge, enabling deeper integration than multi-platform tools.

**Q: How long does setup take? Do I need technical skills?**
Under 10 minutes, no coding required. Install from the Shopify App Store, and Chatty automatically syncs your product catalog. Customize your AI's personality and add any specific FAQs, then go live. If you need help, our support team will even set up your chatbot for you — that's included in every plan. Decathlon trained 10,000 products in a single overnight sync.

**Q: Can I see how much revenue AI generates?**
Yes. Chatty tracks AI-assisted conversations that lead to purchases. You can see your chat-to-sale conversion rate, total AI-assisted revenue, and how many conversations AI handled vs. humans — all in your analytics dashboard. Yoeleo Bike saw $29,586 in AI-assisted revenue in their first month. Happy Hair Brush achieved an 18.75% chat-to-sale conversion rate.

**Q: What channels does Chatty support?**
Chatty unifies five channels in one inbox: **website live chat, WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Instagram, and email.** AI works across all channels, and your team can manage everything from a single dashboard. Available on desktop and mobile apps (iOS/Android) so you can respond on the go.

**Q: What happens when AI can't answer a question?**
Chatty automatically detects when it's not confident in an answer and seamlessly transfers the conversation to a human agent. Your team receives the full chat history so the customer never has to repeat themselves. You can also set specific topics or keywords that always route to humans. It's AI + human working together — not AI replacing your team.

**Q: Is there a free plan?**
Yes. Chatty's free plan includes 50 AI conversations, 100 products for AI training, live chat with 1 team member, unlimited FAQs, and basic analytics. It's a great way to experience the product. Paid plans start at $19.99/month with a 7-day free trial and 30-day money-back guarantee, so there's no risk to try it.
