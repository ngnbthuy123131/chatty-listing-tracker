## Page Design Notes (For Design Team)

1. **Hero section:** Full-width. Dark or bold gradient background. Large headline. Single CTA button (high contrast). Social proof bar directly below.
2. **Problem section:** Clean white background. Left-aligned text. Let the copy breathe — no distracting graphics here. This is where the merchant recognizes themselves.
3. **Competitor comparison:** Use the table. Make Chatty's column visually distinct (brand color highlight). Keep it scannable.
4. **Features section:** Card layout. One benefit per card. Use icons, not feature names. Lead with what the merchant gets, not what the tech does.
5. **Proof section:** Large numbers. Bold stats. Consider an animated counter on scroll.
6. **Setup section:** Numbered steps with simple illustrations or screenshots. Emphasize speed and simplicity.
7. **Final CTA:** Full-width section with strong background color. Single button. Urgency in the copy but not fake urgency — the urgency is real (they're losing sales right now).
8. **Mobile-first:** Every section should be scannable on mobile. Short paragraphs. Large tap targets on CTAs.
