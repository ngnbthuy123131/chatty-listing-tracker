# Chatty Marketing Feature List

> **Purpose:** Feature reference for marketing team — which features to highlight, to whom, and why.
> **Positioning:** AI Sales Assistant for Shopify merchants. Sales-first, not support-first.
> **Last Updated:** February 2026

---

## ICP Segments

| Segment | Revenue | Team Size | Target Plan | Core Need |
|---------|---------|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| **Growing DTC** (PRIMARY) | $700K–$2M | 2–10, 2–3 CS staff | Pro ($68.99/mo) | Sell more, prove ROI |
| **Scaling Mid-Market** | $2M–$5M | 10–20, dedicated CS | Plus ($199/mo) | Scale CS without hiring |
| **Emerging Merchants** | $300K–$700K | 1–3, owner does CS | Basic ($19.99/mo) | Free up owner time |

**Growing DTC** — Pain: slow responses kill conversions, 70% cart abandonment. Must have: revenue attribution, setup < 4 hours, brand voice control. They switch to whichever tool shows ROI first.

**Scaling Mid-Market** — Pain: CS team can't scale, data fragmented. Must have: 80%+ AI resolution rate, deep integrations. They evaluate on "does it fit my stack?"

**Emerging Merchants** — Pain: owner does everything, missing off-hours sales. Must have: low price, 10-min setup, 24/7 availability. They buy the cheapest viable option.

## What Converts Each Segment

### Growing DTC → Pro Plan

| # | Feature | Why It Converts |
|---|---------|-----------------|
| 1 | Revenue Attribution | #1 must-have. They switch to whichever tool shows ROI first. |
| 2 | AI Accuracy + Industry Scenarios | One wrong recommendation = churn. Pre-built apparel scenarios build trust. |
| 3 | Setup < 4 Hours | Can't see value in a single sitting → they abandon. |
| 4 | Flat-Rate Pricing | $68.99/mo flat vs. credit-based competitors = budget predictability. |
| 5 | Smart Handoff | Addresses #1 fear: "AI will damage my brand." |

**Key objection:** "Will AI match my brand voice?" → Brand Voice Control + Scenario Instructions.

### Scaling Mid-Market → Plus Plan

| # | Feature | Why It Converts |
|---|---------|-----------------|
| 1 | 80%+ AI Resolution Rate | Justifies not hiring another CS agent ($40–50K/yr saved). |
| 2 | Integration Ecosystem | Klaviyo, Zendesk, Joy, Shopify Flow are table stakes. |
| 3 | Agent Performance Reports | Managers need team metrics. #1 complaint in competitor reviews. |
| 4 | Unified Multi-Channel Inbox | Solves fragmentation across 3–4 channels. |
| 5 | Unlimited Team Members | Per-agent pricing (Gorgias model) is a dealbreaker. |

**Key objection:** "Can it handle our volume?" → Conversation limits + $0.40 overage flexibility.

### Emerging Merchants → Basic Plan

| # | Feature | Why It Converts |
|---|---------|-----------------|
| 1 | Price ($19.99/mo) | Must be the cheapest viable option. |
| 2 | 10-Minute Setup | Solo merchants won't spend 4 hours. |
| 3 | 24/7 AI Availability | "Stop missing sales while you sleep." |
| 4 | FAQ Page Builder | Reduces inbound at source. Zero-effort value. |
| 5 | Free Plan Available | Removes all risk to start. |

**Key objection:** "Is it worth $20/mo for my small store?" → Time saved (10+ hrs/week) + after-hours sales.
