# Chatty Gap Analysis — Helpdesk & Support Platforms
*Updated: Q1 2026*

## Executive Summary

Chatty is currently an **AI chatbot and live chat tool** — not a helpdesk. Against dedicated helpdesk platforms (Gorgias, eDesk, Re:amaze, Richpanel), Chatty has zero helpdesk capabilities: no ticketing system, no email support workflows, no SLA tracking, no agent performance analytics, and no order management actions within conversations. However, Chatty holds structural advantages that competitors cannot easily replicate: the highest Shopify rating (4.9★), flat-rate pricing that becomes more attractive as AI scales, a sales-first positioning unique in a support-first market, and a 300K+ merchant ecosystem for cross-selling. **The strategic question is not whether to build a helpdesk — it's whether to build a helpdesk that leapfrogs the current generation by being AI-native from day one**, turning the "late entrant" disadvantage into a "clean slate" advantage.

## 3. Vision

**Current Identity:** Chatty is an AI chatbot and live chat tool that helps Shopify merchants sell more through conversational commerce.

**Proposed Vision:** Chatty is the AI-native commerce platform that unifies sales and support for Shopify merchants — making every customer conversation generate revenue, whether pre-sale or post-sale.

**Vision Pillars:**

1. **AI-Native Helpdesk** — Build the first helpdesk designed from scratch around AI, where autonomous resolution is the default and human agents handle only what AI cannot. Unlike competitors who bolt AI onto legacy ticketing, Chatty starts with AI and adds human workflows around it.

2. **Revenue-Generating Support** — Every support interaction is an opportunity to save a sale, upsell, or create loyalty. Revenue attribution, product recommendations, and proactive engagement are embedded in the helpdesk — not siloed in a separate product.

3. **Shopify-Deep, Merchant-Simple** — The deepest Shopify integration in the helpdesk market (order actions, subscription management, Shopify Flow), wrapped in the simplest UX. Complexity lives under the hood; the merchant sees a 10-minute setup.

4. **Predictable, Fair Pricing** — Flat-rate pricing that rewards growth instead of penalizing it. No per-ticket taxes, no AI resolution surcharges, no channel add-on fees. As AI handles more, merchants pay less per interaction — not more.

5. **Ecosystem Power** — Leverage the Avada ecosystem (300K+ merchants, 15+ apps) as a built-in distribution channel and cross-product intelligence layer that no standalone helpdesk can replicate.

## 4. Strategy

### Strategy 1: "AI-First Helpdesk" — Build the Foundation
**Priority:** P0 | **Timeline:** 0–6 months

**Why it matters:** Without a ticketing system, Chatty cannot compete in the helpdesk market. But building a *traditional* helpdesk would be a losing strategy — Gorgias has 8+ years of head start. Instead, build an AI-native helpdesk where the AI Agent is primary and human workflows wrap around it.

**Specific actions:**
- Build a ticket management system (email → ticket, status workflow, assignment, tagging, internal notes)
- Extend existing AI chatbot to handle email/ticket resolution autonomously
- Add AI-drafted responses for agents when AI confidence is low
- Implement round-robin auto-assignment with basic rules engine
- Build agent workspace with customer order sidebar (full Shopify order data)
- Ship basic helpdesk analytics (FRT, resolution time, tickets by status/agent/channel)

### Strategy 2: "Shopify Action Center" — Deep Commerce Integration
**Priority:** P1 | **Timeline:** 3–9 months

**Why it matters:** Gorgias's deepest moat is in-ticket order actions. Chatty must match this to be considered a viable alternative. Being Shopify-exclusive is an advantage here — all engineering effort goes into one platform.

**Specific actions:**
- Build in-conversation order actions: view full order history, refund (full/partial), cancel, edit address
- Integrate Recharge for subscription management (view, pause, cancel, skip)
- Build self-service order tracking portal (customer-facing, embedded on store)
- Add AI-powered WISMO resolution (auto-reply with tracking info)
- Implement Shopify Flow triggers for helpdesk events (new ticket, resolved, escalated)

### Strategy 3: "Integration Velocity" — Ecosystem Expansion
**Priority:** P1 | **Timeline:** 3–12 months

**Why it matters:** Merchants evaluate helpdesks partly on whether their existing tools connect. Chatty's ~5 integrations vs. Gorgias's 100+ is a dealbreaker for many prospects.

**Specific actions:**
- Prioritize integrations by merchant demand: Recharge, Loop Returns, AfterShip, Yotpo, Smile.io, ShipStation
- Build Zapier/Make connector (unlocks thousands of indirect integrations at low effort)
- Add Slack integration for ticket notifications
- Build webhook system for custom workflows
- Create REST API (read-only initially, then write) for mid-market merchants
- Target 30+ integrations by end of year 1

### Strategy 4: "Revenue Intelligence" — Analytics That Prove ROI
**Priority:** P2 | **Timeline:** 6–12 months

**Why it matters:** Chatty's sales-first positioning demands analytics that prove support generates revenue. Currently, analytics are the most-criticized weakness. Building best-in-class revenue analytics reinforces the core differentiator.

**Specific actions:**
- Build comprehensive helpdesk analytics dashboard (FRT, resolution time, agent performance, ticket volume trends)
- Enhance revenue attribution: track which conversations led to orders, upsells, saved carts, retained subscribers
- Add channel performance breakdown (chat vs. email vs. social vs. self-service)
- Implement CSAT survey enhancements with revenue correlation
- Build exportable reports (CSV, scheduled email reports)
- Add SLA tracking and compliance reporting

### Strategy 5: "Migrate & Conquer" — Win Competitor Customers
**Priority:** P2 | **Timeline:** 9–15 months

**Why it matters:** Gorgias's pricing backlash (double-billing, "success tax"), eDesk's price increases, and Re:amaze's stagnation create a migration window. Make switching painless.

**Specific actions:**
- Build one-click data migration from Gorgias, Zendesk, Freshdesk, Re:amaze (ticket history, macros, rules)
- Create comparison landing pages targeting pain points (pricing, billing complexity)
- Offer white-glove migration assistance (leveraging existing support team strength)
- Match Richpanel's "30% savings guarantee" positioning against Gorgias
- Publish transparent pricing calculator showing total cost vs. competitors (including AI fees, add-ons)

## 5. Use Cases That Must Be Resolved

### Use Case 1: "Where Is My Order?" (WISMO)
- **Current state:** Chatty can show tracking via DHL/FedEx/UPS integration but cannot auto-resolve WISMO tickets from email; no ticket system exists
- **Target state:** AI auto-resolves 80%+ of WISMO queries across chat AND email by pulling real-time tracking data and responding autonomously; remaining escalated to agents with full order context
- **Why urgent:** WISMO represents 30-50% of all eCommerce support volume; solving this alone justifies a helpdesk purchase

### Use Case 2: Multi-Agent Support Team
- **Current state:** Chatty supports up to unlimited agents but lacks assignment logic, internal notes, collision detection, and performance tracking — unusable for teams >3 agents
- **Target state:** Round-robin assignment, rules-based routing, internal notes, agent performance dashboards, SLA timers — fully functional for teams of 5-50 agents
- **Why urgent:** Merchants outgrow Chatty at ~3 agents and switch to Gorgias; this is the #1 churn driver for growing brands

### Use Case 3: Returns & Refunds
- **Current state:** Chatty cannot process refunds, initiate returns, or connect to returns platforms (Loop, AfterShip Returns)
- **Target state:** AI auto-processes simple refunds/returns via Shopify and Loop Returns integration; agents handle exceptions with one-click refund in conversation
- **Why urgent:** Returns are the #2 support volume driver after WISMO; Gorgias's in-ticket refund is its most-praised feature

### Use Case 4: Subscription Management
- **Current state:** No subscription platform integrations; Chatty cannot view or manage subscriptions
- **Target state:** Recharge/Bold integration allows AI to answer subscription questions, and agents to pause/cancel/skip/swap from within conversations
- **Why urgent:** Subscription brands have highest ARPU ($200-500/mo per account); losing them to Gorgias due to missing Recharge integration is expensive

### Use Case 5: Email-First Support
- **Current state:** Basic email channel exists but no email helpdesk workflows (threading, signatures, forwarding rules, auto-categorization)
- **Target state:** Full email helpdesk with AI auto-triage, auto-response for common queries, agent assignment, and professional email handling (custom domain, signatures, templates)
- **Why urgent:** Email is still 40-60% of eCommerce support volume; chat-only tools get rejected by brands needing email support

### Use Case 6: Support Manager Dashboard
- **Current state:** Basic analytics only; no agent-level performance data, no FRT/resolution time tracking, no exportable reports
- **Target state:** Real-time dashboard showing team performance, individual agent metrics, ticket trends, SLA compliance, and revenue impact — exportable as CSV and scheduled email reports
- **Why urgent:** Support managers cannot justify Chatty to leadership without proving team performance and ROI

## 7. What Chatty Should NOT Do

| Trap | Why Avoid | What to Do Instead |
|---|---|---|
| **Build for non-Shopify platforms (BigCommerce, WooCommerce)** in the first 18 months | Dilutes engineering focus; Chatty's moat is Shopify depth, not breadth; eDesk proves multi-platform spreads resources thin at small scale | Go deeper on Shopify — be 2x better than Gorgias on Shopify rather than 0.5x as good on 4 platforms |
| **Compete on marketplace integrations (Amazon, eBay, Walmart)** | eDesk owns this niche with 300+ native integrations and Amazon/Walmart developer council seats; years of API work required | Refer marketplace-heavy merchants to eDesk; focus on DTC/Shopify-first merchants |
| **Build per-ticket or per-resolution pricing** | Gorgias's biggest customer complaint is unpredictable billing; per-ticket pricing creates "success tax" anxiety | Keep flat-rate pricing as a core differentiator; it becomes more valuable as AI handles more |
| **Chase enterprise before nailing SMB** | Re:amaze tried to stay small, Richpanel tried to go up-market too early; both stalled; Gorgias succeeded by dominating SMB first | Win the $10K-$100K/month merchant segment first; enterprise features can follow |
| **Build a generic helpdesk** | Zendesk, Freshdesk, and Help Scout already own generic helpdesk; there's no room for another horizontal player | Build an AI-native eCommerce helpdesk — every feature should assume Shopify data and eCommerce context |
| **Charge separately for AI features** | eDesk charges per-resolution for AI; Gorgias double-bills AI ($0.90 + ticket count); both generate backlash | Include AI in all plans; make AI the default, not the add-on — this is Chatty's structural pricing advantage |
| **Overbuild reporting before core helpdesk works** | Gorgias reports are gated behind Pro ($360/mo); building analytics before the helpdesk is solid wastes engineering time | Ship basic analytics in Phase 1, comprehensive analytics in Phase 4; analytics without underlying data is useless |
