## 3. PHÂN LOẠI THEO QUY MÔ DOANH THU (GMV/Revenue)

### STARTER/HOBBY (<$10K/tháng)
**Recommended:** Help Desk Pro ($69/agent) + Self-Service Base (Free)
**Monthly Cost:** ~$69-138 (1-2 agents)

**Phù hợp vì:**
- Price-sensitive → Base plan free, Pro plan affordable
- Cần simplicity → All-in-one inbox, easy setup
- Low order volume → 1,000 orders/mo đủ dùng

**Features cần thiết:**
- Email + Live Chat + Social channels
- Basic automation rules
- Sidekick AI (included)
- Order tracking self-service

**Gaps/Thiếu:**
- SMS channel (cần Pro Max)
- Multi-language (cần Pro Max)

---

### SMB ($10K-$100K/tháng)
**Recommended:** Help Desk Pro ($69/agent) + Self-Service Pro ($120/mo)
**Monthly Cost:** ~$258-465 (2-5 agents)

**Phù hợp vì:**
- Bắt đầu cần automation → Full automation rules included
- Growth-focused → AI features help scale without hiring
- 3,500 orders/mo phù hợp với growth stage

**Features cần thiết:**
- All channels including WhatsApp & Phone
- Full automation capabilities
- AI-assisted replies (Sidekick)
- Revenue tracking
- Change shipping address flow
- Returns & Exchanges automation

**Gaps/Thiếu:**
- Multi-brand support (cần Pro Max)
- Advanced analytics (cần Pro Max)

- Custom integrations

## 9. SUMMARY CHO CHATTY POSITIONING

### Richpanel Strengths (Learn from):
1. **Self-service portal** - Interactive, action-based (not just FAQ)
2. **Fixed pricing** - Predictable costs, good for DTC margins
3. **AI included** - Sidekick AI free with all plans
4. **Guarantee** - 30% automation or refund
5. **Migration** - Free white-glove service

### Richpanel Weaknesses (Opportunity for Chatty):
1. **No vertical-specific features** - Chatty có thể differentiate
2. **Weak B2B/Wholesale** - Gap in market
3. **Limited marketplace integration** - No Amazon/eBay
4. **Self-service là add-on** - Expensive total cost
5. **No Vietnamese market focus** - Chatty advantage in SEA

### Pricing Insights:
- **Total cost for SMB:** $189-465/month (2-5 agents)
- **Total cost for Mid-market:** $855-1,350/month (5-10 agents)
- **Self-service is EXPENSIVE add-on** - $120-360/month on top of help desk
