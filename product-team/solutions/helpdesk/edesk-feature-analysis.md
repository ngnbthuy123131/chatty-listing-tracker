- **Company**: eDesk (formerly xSellco) - Acquired by PayPal

## 9. KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR CHATTY POSITIONING

### eDesk Strengths to Learn From:
1. **Native marketplace integrations** - 300+ channels là moat lớn
2. **AI resolution-based pricing** - Transparent, aligned with value
3. **SLA tracking** - Critical for marketplace sellers
4. **Pre-sales focus** - Turning support into revenue

### eDesk Weaknesses Chatty Can Exploit:
1. **No free tier** - Entry barrier for starters
2. **Mobile app limited** - Re:amaze có mobile mạnh hơn
3. **B2B weak** - Opportunity nếu target wholesale
4. **Complex pricing** - Per-agent + add-ons confusing
5. **No ticket-based pricing** - Dropshipping/high-volume sellers bị disadvantage

### Recommended Chatty Differentiation:
- **Focus on Shopify-first** (compete with Gorgias, not eDesk)
- **Simpler pricing** (all-inclusive tiers)
- **Vietnamese market** (localization advantage)
- **AI-first approach** (not AI as add-on)
