## 7. RECOMMENDATIONS FOR CHATTY (Joy/Avada)

### Gaps to Exploit:
1. **Native Shopify Integration** - Manychat removed theirs
2. **Predictable Pricing** - Manychat scales unpredictably
3. **Helpdesk Features** - Manychat is marketing-first
4. **B2B Capabilities** - Underserved by Manychat
5. **Vietnamese Market** - Local support/pricing
6. **Bundled AI** - Manychat charges $29 extra

### Features to Match:
1. Visual Flow Builder quality
2. Comment-to-DM automation
3. Multi-channel (IG, FB, WhatsApp)
4. Growth Tools variety
5. Basic AI intent recognition
