# Respond.io: Nen tang quan ly hoi thoai khach hang Omnichannel hang dau Dong Nam A

**Respond.io la mot nen tang quan ly hoi thoai khach hang (Customer Conversation Management) duoc thanh lap nam 2017 tai Hong Kong, hien co tru so chinh tai Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.** Voi **$18.5M ARR** (GetLatka, 2025), **10,000+ doanh nghiep** su dung tai **85+ quoc gia**, va tong vong goi von **$8.8M** (Series A: $7M do Headline dan dau), Respond.io da tro thanh mot trong nhung "player" lon nhat trong thi truong omnichannel messaging tai khu vuc Chau A - Thai Binh Duong. Diem manh cot loi cua Respond.io la kha nang hop nhat tat ca cac kenh giao tiep (WhatsApp, Messenger, Instagram, Telegram, Email, Voice) vao mot inbox duy nhat, ket hop voi AI Agent va workflow automation manh me. **Doi voi Chatty, Respond.io la doi thu canh tranh gian tiep - ho tap trung vao B2C enterprise voi gia cao ($79-$279/thang), trong khi Chatty phuc vu SMB tren Shopify voi gia re hon ($0-$199/thang).**

## 2. San pham va Cong nghe

### Cac module san pham chinh

Respond.io la mot nen tang Customer Conversation Management (CCM) toan dien, bao gom:

1. **Omnichannel Inbox**: Hop nhat tat ca tin nhan tu WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Instagram, Telegram, WeChat, LINE, Email, SMS, Website Chat va Voice vao mot giao dien duy nhat
2. **AI Agents**: Cac agent AI co the tra loi tu dong, qualify lead, dat lich hen, cap nhat CRM, va chuyen giao cho nhan vien khi can
3. **Workflows Automation**: No-code workflow builder cho phep tu dong hoa quy trinh phuc tap
4. **Broadcasts**: Gui tin nhan hang loat qua nhieu kenh
5. **Contact Management**: Quan ly va phan khuc khach hang
6. **Reports & Analytics**: Bao cao hieu suat doi ngu va hieu qua chien dich
