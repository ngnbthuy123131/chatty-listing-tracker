# Wati: Nen tang WhatsApp Business API hang dau cho SMB voi $35M funding tu Tiger Global va Shopify

**Wati (WhatsApp Team Inbox) la mot trong nhung nen tang WhatsApp Business API lon nhat the gioi, duoc dau tu boi Tiger Global, DST Global va Shopify voi tong $35M funding.** Duoc thanh lap nam 2020 boi Bianca Ho va Ken Yeung tai Hong Kong, Wati da nhanh chong vuon len vi tri dan dau trong thi truong WhatsApp Business Solution Provider (BSP) cho SMB, phuc vu **8,000+ doanh nghiep** tai **160+ quoc gia**. Voi doanh thu uoc tinh **$9.6M (10/2024)** va doi ngu **156+ nhan vien**, Wati dinh vi la giai phap "no-code" de doanh nghiep nho va vua tiep can WhatsApp API ma khong can ky thuat phuc tap. Tuy nhien, mo hinh gia per-conversation va markup fee khien tong chi phi thuc te cao hon nhieu so voi gia niem yet — mot diem khac biet lon so voi Chatty's flat-rate pricing.

## 2. San pham va cong nghe

### Kien truc san pham

Wati la mot **WhatsApp Business Solution Provider (BSP)** duoc Meta uy quyen, cung cap nen tang de doanh nghiep su dung WhatsApp API ma khong can tu xay dung ha tang. San pham gom cac module chinh:

**1. Shared Team Inbox**
- Hop thu chung cho nhieu agent xu ly tin nhan WhatsApp
- Smart routing, canned responses, data tagging
- Ho tro omnichannel (WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, web chat)

**2. No-Code Chatbot Builder**
- Drag-and-drop flow builder voi toi da 200 buoc
- Trigger theo keyword, action, hoac dieu kien
- Ho tro NLP (Natural Language Processing)

**3. Wati Knowbot (AI Chatbot)**
- Su dung ChatGPT, GPT-4 Turbo va cac mo hinh OpenAI
- Train tu file PDF (toi da 200MB) va website URLs
- Pro plan: 20 URLs; Business plan: 50 URLs
- Chi ho tro WhatsApp messages (chua ho tro kenh khac)

**4. Broadcast & Campaign Manager**
- Gui tin nhan hang loat theo template da duoc Meta phe duyet
- Phan khuc doi tuong, scheduling, A/B testing
- Theo doi open rate, click-through rate

**5. E-commerce Integrations**
- **Shopify**: Order confirmation, abandoned cart recovery, shipping updates
- **WhatsApp Pay**: Thanh toan truc tiep trong WhatsApp
- **Product Catalog**: Hien thi san pham tu Shopify trong WhatsApp

### Mo hinh gia

Wati su dung mo hinh **subscription + usage-based pricing** — mot cau truc phuc tap hon nhieu so voi flat-rate cua Chatty.

| Goi | Gia thang (USD) | Gia nam (USD/thang) | Users | Chatbot Sessions/thang |
|-----|-----------------|---------------------|-------|------------------------|
| **Growth** | $69 | $59 (~15% off) | 5 | 1,000 |
| **Pro** | $149 | $119 (~20% off) | 5 | 2,000 |
| **Business** | $349 | $279 (~20% off) | 5 | 5,000 |

**Chi phi bo sung:**
- **Additional users**: ~$25-30/user/thang
- **Conversation markup**: Wati tinh them ~20% markup tren gia cua Meta
- **Shopify integration**: Phi rieng (khong ro rang)
- **Extra chatbot sessions**: Phi theo goi

**Luu y quan trong (tu 01/07/2025):** Meta chuyen tu Conversation-Based Pricing sang Message-Based Pricing. Dieu nay co nghia la phi se tinh theo so tin nhan gui di, khong phai theo conversation — co the lam tang chi phi dang ke cho cac doanh nghiep gui nhieu tin nhan trong mot cuoc hoi thoai.

## 10. Danh gia chien luoc

### SWOT Analysis

| **Diem manh** | **Diem yeu** |
|---------------|--------------|
| WhatsApp BSP chinh thuc, official partner | 100% phu thuoc Meta/WhatsApp platform |
| $35M funding tu Tiger Global, Shopify, DST | Pricing phuc tap, markup fee gay mat trust |
| 8,000+ customers, 160+ countries | Shopify app rating thap (3.7 stars) |
| Strong presence tai emerging markets | Support quality complaints tang |
| No-code chatbot builder de su dung | AI features (Knowbot) chua manh bang doi thu |
| Shopify la investor (strategic advantage) | Chua profitable, dang burn cash |

| **Co hoi** | **Thach thuc** |
|------------|----------------|
| LATAM va India la thi truong WhatsApp lon nhat | Respond.io, Zoko, Interakt canh tranh gia |
| AI advancements co the tao moat | Meta co the thay doi chinh sach BSP bat ky luc nao |
| Shopify partnership co the deepen | Message-based pricing (07/2025) tang chi phi users |
| Multi-channel expansion | Yellow.ai va enterprise players move downmarket |

### Tai sao Wati thanh cong

1. **First-mover advantage trong WhatsApp BSP cho SMB** — Wati la mot trong nhung player dau tien tap trung hoan toan vao SMB voi self-service model, trong khi doi thu (Twilio, MessageBird) focus enterprise.

2. **Timing hoan hao** — Launch 2020 trung voi COVID-19 day SMB len digital va WhatsApp tro thanh kenh customer service chinh tai emerging markets.

3. **Geographic focus dung** — Tap trung vao India, Brazil, SEA — nhung noi WhatsApp la "SMS cua thoi dai moi" va SMB san sang tra phi cho API access.

4. **Strong investor backing** — Tiger Global, Shopify, DST Global cung cap khong chi von ma con network va credibility.

5. **Product-led distribution** — Free trial 7 ngay, no-code setup, va content marketing manh giup acquire customers voi CAC thap.

### Danh gia giai doan cong ty

Wati dang o giai doan **Growth Stage** — da co product-market fit, dang scale nhanh, nhung chua profitable. Voi $35M funding va burn rate uoc tinh $300-500K/thang, runway con 3-5 nam. Cong ty can chung minh co the tang revenue nhanh hon burn de dat profitability hoac raise them vong moi.

### Goc nhin doi thu: "Neu la doi thu, ban se tan cong dau?"

1. **Pricing transparency** — Tao mo hinh gia flat-rate don gian, khong markup, khong hidden fees. Day la diem yeu lon nhat cua Wati.
