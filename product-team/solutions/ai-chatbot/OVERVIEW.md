# AI Chatbot & Live Chat — Competitive Overview

*Updated: Q1 2026*

## Landscape Summary

Five Shopify AI chatbot products researched. The market splits into two tiers: **Tidio** dominates by scale ($20-35M ARR, 300K+ businesses), while four Shopify-native players compete for the fast-growing SMB segment. All are converging on "AI that sells, not just supports" — the positioning Chatty pioneered.

## Key Differentiators by Product

**Chatty** — Lowest price, highest review count, flat-rate pricing advantage. Only product where AI cost doesn't scale with usage. Avada ecosystem (300K merchants) for cross-sell.

**Tidio** — Scale leader. Lyro AI at 67% resolution rate. Multi-platform (Shopify, WooCommerce, WordPress). Conversation-based billing. 10 free operator seats.

**Rep AI** — Proprietary behavioral AI ("Rescue Algorithm") detects exit intent from 500+ data points. Sales concierge, not a chatbot. Premium pricing ($299+) targets mid-market.

**Manifest AI** — AI Quiz engine (unique), PDP widgets embedded on product pages, built-in A/B testing. Y Combinator + Sequoia backed. Pivot from failed parent (Bikayi).

**VanChat** — Perfect 5.0 rating. Dual AI (GPT-4o + Claude 3). Claims 97% accuracy. Add-to-cart inside chat. Early-stage, smallest install base.

## Threats to Watch

1. **Tidio Lyro Connect** — Selling AI agent into Zendesk/Intercom users expands their TAM beyond direct competition
2. **Rep AI behavioral AI** — If exit-intent prediction proves out, it's a genuine moat Chatty can't easily replicate
3. **VanChat's perfect rating** — At 5.0★ with "Built for Shopify" badge, could become a rating threat as they grow
4. **Shopify native AI** — Shopify Inbox + Sidekick could commoditize basic chat for all players

## Opportunities for Chatty

1. **Pricing narrative** — Chatty is the only flat-rate player. As AI handles more conversations, competitors get more expensive while Chatty stays flat. This story strengthens over time.
2. **Review moat** — 1,735+ reviews is 3-16x more than competitors (except Tidio). Hard to replicate. Keep the white-glove support engine running.
3. **Revenue attribution** — Shared with Gorgias but rare in chatbot category. Double down on "AI that sells" with provable ROI metrics.
4. **Avada cross-sell** — 300K+ merchants across Joy, Air Reviews, SEO Suite. No competitor has this built-in distribution.
5. **Mid-market gap** — Between Chatty's $199 Plus and Rep AI's $299+ there's room for an enterprise-lite tier targeting Shopify Plus merchants.
