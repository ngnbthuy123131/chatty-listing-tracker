## 6. COMPETITIVE POSITIONING SUMMARY

### Strengths
1. **AI-First Approach:** Lyro powered by Claude (Anthropic) - không hallucinate
2. **Ecommerce Focus:** Deep Shopify integration, product recommendations
3. **Easy Setup:** No-code flows, quick deployment
4. **Conversation-Based Pricing:** Fair for low-volume
5. **All-in-One:** Chat + Ticketing + AI + Flows trong 1 platform
6. **SMB-Friendly:** Generous free tier, affordable entry

### Weaknesses
1. **Scaling Cost:** Expensive at high volume (conversation-based)
2. **Limited Enterprise Features:** SSO, compliance chỉ ở Premium
3. **No Voice Support:** Chat/text only
4. **Basic Ticketing:** Không mạnh bằng Zendesk/Gorgias
5. **Limited Integrations:** Không nhiều native integrations
6. **Actions Complexity:** Cần developer cho advanced automation

### Target Customer Profile
- **Size:** SMB to Mid-market (< $1M/tháng GMV)
- **Industry:** Ecommerce, đặc biệt Shopify stores
- **Team Size:** 1-20 support agents
- **Technical Level:** Low to Medium
- **Primary Need:** AI automation + Live chat
