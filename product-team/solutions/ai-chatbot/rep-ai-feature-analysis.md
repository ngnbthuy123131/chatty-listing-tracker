## 5. PHÂN LOẠI THEO MÔ HÌNH KINH DOANH

### DTC (Direct-to-Consumer)
**Core Target Segment của Rep AI**

**Highly Relevant Features:**
- Brand voice customization (brand control)
- AI Personality design
- Custom branding (widget appearance)
- Marketing integrations (Klaviyo, Postscript, Attentive)
- Customer insights & data ownership
- Conversion analytics

**ROI Metrics Claimed:**
- 12%+ Conversion Rate
- 16%+ Higher AOV
- 20x+ ROI

### Dropshipping
**Partial Fit**

**Relevant Features:**
- Automated support (high volume, low margin)
- Order tracking (WISMO - critical for dropship)
- Returns handling automation
- Multi-language (often international)

**Limitations:**
- Long shipping times → high support volume → có thể vượt plan limits
- Low margin makes $99-$350/month more painful
- Need Support module → higher cost

### Subscription
**Good Fit**

**Highly Relevant Features:**
- Edit subscriptions skill
- Cancel order handling (retention opportunity)
- Modify order details
- Subscription analytics
- Recharge/Bold không được mention nhưng likely compatible

**Use Cases:**
- Pause/skip subscription qua chat
- Upgrade/downgrade plan
- Retention conversations

### Marketplace Seller
**Poor Fit**

**Limitations:**
- Shopify-only (no Amazon, eBay integration)
- Single-store focus
- No multi-channel inventory sync

### Wholesale/B2B
**Limited Fit**

**Missing Features:**
- Custom pricing/catalogs
- Quote management
- Account-based pricing
- Net terms handling

**Available:**
- Product Q&A (complex catalogs)
- Order tracking
- Basic support automation

### Hybrid
**Moderate Fit**

**Relevant Features:**
- Flexible conversation flows (Flow Studio)
- Custom support policies
- Multi-use case handling
- B2C features strong, B2B features weak
