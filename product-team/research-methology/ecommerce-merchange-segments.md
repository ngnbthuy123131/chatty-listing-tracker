# eCommerce Merchant Segmentation Framework

## 1. Theo Quy Mô Doanh Thu (GMV/Revenue)

Tiêu chí phổ biến nhất vì correlate trực tiếp với willingness to pay và complexity of needs.

| Segment | Yearly GMV | Characteristics |
|---------|------------|-----------------|
| Starter/Hobby | <$120K | Price sensitive, cần simplicity |
| SMB | $120K - $1.2M | Bắt đầu cần automation, growth-focused |
| Mid-market | $1.2M - $12M | Cần scalability, integration, dedicated support |
| Enterprise | >$12M | Custom solutions, SLAs, account management |

---

## 2. Theo Giai Đoạn Phát Triển (Growth Stage)

| Stage | Description | Primary Needs |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| Pre-launch | Đang setup store | Foundation tools, templates |
| Launch | Mới ra mắt | Traffic acquisition, brand awareness |
| Growth | Đang scale | Conversion optimization, retention |
| Mature | Ổn định | Efficiency, margin optimization |
| Declining/Pivoting | Cần turnaround | Cost reduction, new strategies |

---

## 3. Theo Ngành Hàng (Vertical)

| Vertical | Key Characteristics |
|----------|---------------------|
| Fashion & Apparel | High return rates, seasonal, visual-heavy |
| Beauty & Cosmetics | Subscription potential, UGC-driven |
| Food & Beverage | Perishable, local delivery, repeat purchase |
| Electronics | High AOV, comparison shopping, warranty |
| Home & Garden | Seasonal, bulky shipping, long consideration |
| Health & Wellness | Trust-critical, subscription, compliance |
| B2B/Wholesale | Bulk pricing, net terms, custom catalogs |

---

## 4. Theo Mô Hình Kinh Doanh

| Model | Focus Areas |
|-------|-------------|
| DTC (Direct-to-Consumer) | Brand building, margin optimization |
| Dropshipping | Speed, volume, supplier management |
| Subscription | Retention, LTV, churn reduction |
| Marketplace Seller | Multi-channel sync, inventory management |
| Wholesale/B2B | Bulk pricing, account management |
| Hybrid | Flexibility, channel coordination |

---

## 5. Theo Hành Vi Sử Dụng Sản Phẩm (Product Usage)

| Segment | Behavior | Strategy |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Power Users | High engagement, nhiều features | Upsell, advocacy program |
| Casual Users | Basic usage | Education, feature discovery |
| Champions | High NPS, referrals | Referral program, case studies |
| At-risk | Declining usage | Intervention, re-engagement |

---

## 6. Theo Mức Độ Tech Sophistication

| Level | Description | Preferred Experience |
|-------|-------------|---------------------|
| DIY Merchants | Tự làm mọi thứ | No-code, templates, wizards |
| Semi-technical | Basic customization | Guided setup, documentation |
| Tech-savvy | Developer resources | API-first, webhooks, SDK |
| Agency-managed | Outsourced operations | Partner programs, bulk management |

---

## 7. Theo Pain Points / Jobs-to-be-Done

| Primary Pain Point | Typical Profile |
|--------------------|-----------------|
| Traffic Acquisition | New stores, competitive niches |
| Conversion Optimization | High traffic, low conversion |
| Retention & Loyalty | Established stores, commoditized products |
| Operational Efficiency | Scaling stores, thin margins |
| Brand Building | DTC brands, premium positioning |

## Multi-Dimensional Segmentation Matrix

Các platform lớn thường kết hợp nhiều dimensions:

| Dimension | Primary Use Case |
|-----------|------------------|
| Revenue/GMV | Pricing tiers, sales motion |
| Vertical | Feature prioritization, content marketing |
| Growth Stage | Onboarding flow, messaging |
| Tech Sophistication | UX complexity, support approach |
| Business Model | Integration requirements |

---

## Ứng Dụng Thực Tế

### Cho Pricing Strategy
- Starter → Free tier / Freemium
- SMB → Self-serve paid plans
- Mid-market → Annual contracts, volume discounts
- Enterprise → Custom pricing, negotiation

### Cho Sales Motion
- Starter/SMB → Product-led growth (PLG)
- Mid-market → Hybrid (PLG + Sales assist)
- Enterprise → Sales-led, account executives

### Cho Product Development
- Prioritize features theo segment có highest LTV
- Build vertical-specific templates/presets
- Develop API/integrations cho tech-savvy segment

### Cho Marketing & Content
- Tailor messaging theo growth stage
- Create vertical-specific case studies
- Segment email campaigns theo usage behavior
